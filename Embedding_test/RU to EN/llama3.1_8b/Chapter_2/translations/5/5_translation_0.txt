He repeated to himself, standing in the middle of the room, "Pieces of torn hem won't arouse any suspicion; it seems so, it seems so!" and with a painful attention began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere, if he hadn't forgotten something else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple reasoning left him, was starting to torment him unbearably. "What, is it really starting, is this really the execution arriving? Look, look, it's so!" Indeed, the scraps of braid which he had cut off from his pants were lying on the floor, in the middle of the room, for anyone to see first! "What's happening to me?" - he shouted again as if lost. Then a strange idea came to him: maybe all his clothes are bloody, maybe there are many stains, but he doesn't see them, doesn't notice them because his reasoning has weakened, is fragmented... his mind is clouded... Suddenly he remembered that the wallet was also bloody. "Ah! So it means that there must be blood in the pocket too, because I put a wet wallet into my pocket then!" He turned out his pocket at once and - yes, there were stains on the lining of the pocket! "So it means I haven't lost all reason completely, so there is still reasoning and memory, since I remembered myself and figured it out! - he thought with triumph, taking a deep breath with his whole chest, - just a weak moment from fever, a delirium for a minute", and he pulled the lining out of the left pocket of his pants.
