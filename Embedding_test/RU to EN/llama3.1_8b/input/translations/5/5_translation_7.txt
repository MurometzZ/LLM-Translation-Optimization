"Rips of torn fabric won't arouse any suspicions; it seems so, it seems so!" he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with a painful attention began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere, if he hadn't forgotten something else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple reasoning left him, started to torment him unbearably. "What, is it really starting, is this really the execution approaching? Look, look, it's true!" Indeed, the scraps of braid which he had cut off from his pants were lying on the floor, in the middle of the room, so that anyone could see them first! "What's happening to me?" he cried out again like a lost person. Then a strange idea came into his head: what if all his clothes are bloody, what if there are many stains, but he just doesn't see or notice them because his reasoning has weakened, is fragmented... his mind is clouded... Suddenly he remembered that the wallet was also bloody. "Ah! So it means there must be blood in the pocket too, because I stuffed a wet wallet into my pocket then!" He immediately turned out the pocket and - yes, it's true - on the lining of the pocket there are stains, spots! "So it means he hasn't left his reason completely, so there is still reasoning and memory, since he himself realized and thought of it! - he thought with triumph, deeply and joyfully breathing in all his chest - just a weak moment of feverishness, a delirium for a minute", and he ripped out the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants.
