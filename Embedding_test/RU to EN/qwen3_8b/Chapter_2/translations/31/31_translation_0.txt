And he opened the window from the ditch and stood in the window, squealing like a little pig; and that's a disgrace. And how can one stand in the window to the street, squealing like a little pig; and that's a disgrace. Fui-fui-fui! And Karl dragged him from the window by the coat, and indeed, sir captain, he tore his coat. And then he cried that he had to pay fifteen shillings as a fine. And I myself, sir captain, paid five shillings to him as a fine. And this is an unworthy guest, sir captain, and makes a scandal! I, saying, will be a big satire on you, because I can write everything about you in all the newspapers. — From the writers, meaning? — Yes, sir captain, and what kind of an unworthy guest, sir captain, when in a noble house… — Well, well, well!
