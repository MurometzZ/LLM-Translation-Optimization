"Three hours already!" He sat on the couch—and suddenly everything came back to him! All at once, in an instant, he remembered everything! At first, he thought he was going mad. A terrible cold gripped him; but the cold was also from the fever, which had already begun with him in his sleep. Now, however, a sudden chill struck him so strongly that his teeth almost came loose, and everything inside him was trembling. He opened the door and began to listen: the whole house was completely asleep. He looked at himself in astonishment and everything around him in the room, not understanding: how could he, yesterday, upon entering, not lock the doors and fling himself onto the couch, not only without undressing, but even with his hat on? It had slipped off and lay on the floor, near the pillow. "If someone had come in, what would they have thought? That I was drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to examine himself, from head to toe, his entire attire: were there any traces?
