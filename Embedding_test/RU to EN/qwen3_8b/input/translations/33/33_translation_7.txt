There they are, the writers!—and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. Three days ago, there was an incident in the tavern: he had lunch, but refused to pay; "I'll describe you in satire for that," he said. On the steamship, too, there was another incident, last week: a respected family of a state councillor, his wife and daughter, were insulted with the most offensive words. Yesterday, from the confectionery, he was pushed out. That's what they are, the writers, literati, students, preachers… tsk! Go away! I'll come myself to you… then be careful! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna, with affected politeness, began to bow in all directions, and while bowing, she managed to reach the doors; but she bumped into a prominent officer with an open, fresh face and splendid thick blond curls.
