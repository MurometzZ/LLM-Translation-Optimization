— Yes, Mr. Captain, and what an unworthy guest, Mr. Captain, when in a noble home…  
— Well, well, well! Enough! I've already told you, told you, I've told you…  
— Colonel Ilyich Petrovich! — the clerk spoke again significantly. The lieutenant quickly looked at him; the clerk slightly nodded his head. — …Here you are, esteemed Laviza Ivanovna, my final word, and this is for the last time, — the lieutenant continued. — If there's another scandal in your noble home, I'll take you to the cugundar, as it's said in high style. Did you hear? So a writer, a composer, took five groschen in "The Noble Home" for a fold? There they are, the composers!
