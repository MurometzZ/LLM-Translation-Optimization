"Three hours already!" He sat on the couch—and suddenly everything came back to him! In an instant, he remembered everything! At first, he thought he was going mad. A terrible cold gripped him; but the cold was also from a fever that had already begun with him in his sleep. Now, suddenly, such a chill struck him that his teeth almost came out, and everything inside him was churning. He opened the door and began to listen: everyone in the house was completely asleep. He looked at himself in astonishment and everything around the room, not understanding: how could he, yesterday, upon entering, not lock the doors and run to the couch, not only without undressing, but even with his hat on? It had slipped down and immediately lay on the floor, near the pillow. "What would someone have thought if they had come in? That I'm drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to examine himself, from head to toe, his entire outfit: were there any signs?
