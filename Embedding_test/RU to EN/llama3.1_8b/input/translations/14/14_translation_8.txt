Nastasya, it turned out, couldn't have noticed anything anyway, thank God!" Then he trembled as he unfolded the summons and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was an ordinary summons from the district to appear today at half past ten in the office of the district supervisor. "When could this be? I don't have any dealings with the police myself! And why just today?" he thought, bewildered. "God, let it happen quickly!" He had thrown himself on his knees to pray, but even he laughed at himself - not at the prayer, but at himself. He hastily began to get dressed. "I'll be lost anyway! I'll put on my socks!"
