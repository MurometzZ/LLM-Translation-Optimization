— You, so such and such!— he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the widow lady had already left),— what happened to you last night? Again, shame, making a row on the whole street! Again a fight and drunkenness. Dreaming of peace! I've already told you, I've already warned you ten times that I won't let you go out at eleven! Again and again, you, so such and such! Even the paper fell from Raskolnikov's hands, and he stared wildly at the wealthy lady who was being treated so impolitely; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and immediately this whole incident began to please him very much. He listened with enjoyment, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh… All his nerves were jumping.— Ilya Petrovich!
