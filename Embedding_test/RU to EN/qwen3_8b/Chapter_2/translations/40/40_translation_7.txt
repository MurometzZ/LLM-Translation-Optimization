The call was so strong that he had already risen from his place to carry it out. "Should I think about it for a minute?"—it passed through his mind. "No, better not to think at all and just throw it to the shoulders!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Ilyia Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It's impossible to free both! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourselves: why call a servant if it's their affair? To report on themselves? Or for cunning? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen near the gates by both the servants and the merchant's wife at the very moment he entered: he was walking with three friends, parted from them at the gates, and inquired about the servants' residence while still with his friends. So, would he inquire about the residence if he had such an intention?"
