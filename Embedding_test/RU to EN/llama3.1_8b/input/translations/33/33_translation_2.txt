There they are, the writers! - and he cast a scornful glance at Raskolnikov. - There was an incident in the tavern three days ago: he ate his meal but refused to pay; "I'll write you up in satire for it," he said. On the steamer too, last week, he insulted with vile words a respectable family of a civil servant, his wife and daughter. From the confectionery yesterday they threw out one man who was causing a commotion. That's what writers are like, literati, students, heralds... Ugh! Off you go! I'll come to see you myself... then watch yourself! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna began to curtsy all around and, curtsying, reached the doors; but in the doorway she bumped into one of the prominent officers from behind, with a fresh open face and magnificent thick blond sideburns.
