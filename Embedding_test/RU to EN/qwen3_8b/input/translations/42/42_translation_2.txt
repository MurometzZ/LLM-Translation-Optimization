"No, the matter is very unclear," added Ilyia Petrovich. Raskolnikov raised his hat and went toward the door, but he did not reach the door... 

When he awoke, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that a person was supporting him on his right, that another person was standing on his left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikodim Fomich was standing before him, staring intently at him; he rose from the chair. "What is this, are you ill?" Nikodim Fomich rather sharply asked. "They signed barely with a pen, as if tracing with a feather," noted the scribe, sitting down in his place and resuming his work with the papers. "How long have you been ill?" cried Ilyia Petrovich from his place, also rummaging through the papers.
