"Money? What money? — Raskolnikov thought, — but... surely it's not that!" And he trembled with joy. Suddenly, it became to him terribly, immeasurably easy. Everything fell from his shoulders. — When are you supposed to come, sir? — shouted the officer, increasingly and more inexplicably offended, — You are written to come at nine, but now it's already twelve o'clock! — They brought me a quarter of an hour ago, — Raskolnikov answered loudly from behind, also suddenly and unexpectedly for himself getting angry and even finding some pleasure in it. — It's enough that I came here sick with a fever. — Don't shout!
