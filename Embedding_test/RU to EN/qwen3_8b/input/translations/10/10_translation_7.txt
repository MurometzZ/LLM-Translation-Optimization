His entire room was of such a size that one could take off a hook without getting out of bed. So it is: the janitor and Nastasia are standing there. Nastasia looked at him in a strange way. He stared at the janitor with a defiant and desperate look. He silently handed him a gray, folded-in-half paper sealed with a bottle-seal. — A summons from the office, — he said, handing over the paper. — From which office? — In the police, they call, to the office. Well, which office is it. — To the police!
