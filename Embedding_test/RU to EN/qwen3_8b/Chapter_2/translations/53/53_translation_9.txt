"By God, that's it! — he suddenly thought, seized by inexhaustible wrath. — Well, it's begun, so let it begin, may the devil take the new life! How foolish, Lord!... How much I lied and disgraced myself today! How vilely I bowed and flirted with the worst Ilyá Petrovich! Well, nonsense, even that! I don't care about them all, nor about the fact that I bowed and flirted! Not at all! Not at all!..."
Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question at once confused him and greatly astonished him:
"If indeed all this was done intentionally, not foolishly, if you indeed had a definite and firm goal, then how come you haven't even looked into your wallet so far and don't know what you've gotten, from which all these torments came, and why you deliberately went along with such a base, nasty, low act?"
