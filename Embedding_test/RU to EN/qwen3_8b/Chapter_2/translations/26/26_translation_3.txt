The clerk looked at them with a smile. The hot-headed captain was evidently puzzled. "This isn't your business!" he shouted finally in an unnaturally loud voice, "but you may as well submit the report they require from you. Show him, Alexander Grigoryevich. Complaints about you! Don't pay money! What a bold eagle you've flown!" But Raskolnikov was no longer listening and greedily grabbed the paper, seeking to quickly find the answers. He read it once, twice, and didn't understand.
