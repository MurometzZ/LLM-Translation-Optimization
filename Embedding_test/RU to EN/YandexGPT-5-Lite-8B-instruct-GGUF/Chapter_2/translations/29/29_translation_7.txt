 — And you, you such and such and such, — he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the mourning lady had already left), — what happened to you last night? huh? Shame again, making a racket in the whole street. Fighting and drinking again. You dream about being put in a straitjacket! After all, I've told you, I've warned you ten times that I won't tolerate it the eleventh time! And you again, again, you such and such! Even the paper fell out of Raskolnikov's hands, and he was looking wildly at the stout lady, who was being so unceremoniously handled; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and immediately this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping. — Ilya Petrovich!
