What is this? He immediately rushed to take them out and throw them on the table. Having gathered everything, even turning inside out his pockets to make sure nothing was left, he moved all this pile to the corner. In the very corner, at the bottom, in one spot, the wallpaper that had peeled away from the wall was torn: right away he started stuffing everything into this hole, under the paper: "It's all in! Everything out of sight and the wallet too!" — he happily thought, standing up and staring blankly at the corner, the hole even more gaping. Suddenly he shuddered with fear: "My God," — he whispered in despair, "what has happened to me? Is this hidden? Is this how one hides?" Indeed, he hadn't expected anything; he thought there would be only money, so he hadn't prepared a place in advance — "but now, now what am I happy about?" — he thought. — Is this how one hides?"
