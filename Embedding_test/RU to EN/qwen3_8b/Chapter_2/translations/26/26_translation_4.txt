The clerk looked at them with a smile. The hot-headed captain seemed clearly puzzled. "This isn't your business!" he shouted at last in an unnaturally loud voice, "but you'd better submit the report they're demanding from you. Show him, Alexander Grigoryevich. Complaints about you! Don't pay money! What a bold eagle you've flown!" But Raskolnikov was already not listening and greedily grabbed the paper, seeking to quickly find the solution. He read it once, twice, and didn't understand.
