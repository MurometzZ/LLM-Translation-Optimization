His entire room was so small that you could hook a nail without getting out of bed. And indeed: the janitor and Nastasya are standing there. Nastasya somehow strangely glanced at him. He looked at the janitor with an impudent and desperate expression. The latter silently handed him a grey, folded in half piece of paper sealed with a bottle stamp. — It's a summons from the office, he said, handing over the paper. — From which office?... — To the police, that is, they're calling you to the office. You know what office it is. — To the police!
