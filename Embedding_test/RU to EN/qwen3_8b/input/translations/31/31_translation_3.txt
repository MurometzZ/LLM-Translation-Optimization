And he opened the window in the ditch and climbed in, like a little pig, screaming; and that's a disgrace. And how can one scream in the window to the street, like a little pig, screaming; and that's a disgrace. Ugh-ugh-ugh! And Karl pulled him by the coat from the window, and indeed, sir captain, his rock was broken. And then he shouted that he had to pay fifteen shillings as a fine. And I myself, sir captain, paid five shillings as a fine. And this is an unworthy guest, sir captain, and one who makes a scandal! I, saying, will be a big satirist, having written about you in all the newspapers. — From the writers, then? — Yes, sir captain, and what an unworthy guest, sir captain, when in a noble house…
