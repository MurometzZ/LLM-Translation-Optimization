Nothing and no one; no one had looked in. Even Nastasya hadn't touched anything. But, my God! How could he have left all these things in this hole? He rushed to the corner, stuck his hand under the wallpaper, and started pulling out items, stuffing them into his pockets. In total, there were eight items: two small boxes with earrings or something similar—he hadn't looked carefully; then four small saffron-colored cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper paper. Something else was also wrapped in newspaper paper, seeming like an order…

He placed everything into different pockets, the coat, and the remaining right pants pocket, trying to make it inconspicuous. He also took the wallet along with the items. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it completely open.
