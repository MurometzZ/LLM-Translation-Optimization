He repeated to himself, standing in the middle of the room, "Fragments of torn hem won't arouse any suspicions; it seems so, it seems so!" and with a strained attention began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere, if he hadn't forgotten something else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple reasoning, left him, started to torment him unbearably. "What, is it really starting, is this really the execution arriving? Look, look, it's so!" Indeed, the scraps of braid which he had cut off from his pants were lying on the floor in the middle of the room, for anyone to see! "What's happening with me?" he cried out again like a lost man. Then a strange idea came into his head: what if all his clothes are bloody, what if there are many stains, but he just can't see them, can't notice them because his reasoning has weakened, is fragmented... his mind is clouded... Suddenly he remembered that the wallet was also bloody. "Ah! So it means that there must be blood in the pocket too, because I put a wet wallet into my pocket then!" He immediately turned out the pocket and - yes, indeed - on the lining of the pocket there are stains, spots! "So it means I haven't lost all reason completely, so there is still reasoning and memory, since I remembered myself and figured it out myself!" he thought triumphantly, taking a deep breath with his whole chest. "Just a weak moment from fever, a delirium for one minute," - and he ripped off the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants.
