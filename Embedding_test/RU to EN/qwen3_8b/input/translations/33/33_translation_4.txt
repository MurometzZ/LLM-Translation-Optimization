There they are, the sociologists!—and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. Three days ago, there was an incident in the tavern: he had lunch, but refused to pay; "I'll describe you in satire," he said. Another incident on the ship, last week, a respectable family of a ministerial counselor, his wife and daughter, were insulted with the most offensive words. Yesterday, from the confectionery, I was thrown out for pushing. That's how they are, the sociologists, writers, students, agitators… pah! Go away! I'll come myself… then be careful! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna began to sit down everywhere with exaggerated politeness, and as she sat down, she managed to reach the doors; but she bumped into a prominent officer with an open, fresh face and excellent thick golden curls.
