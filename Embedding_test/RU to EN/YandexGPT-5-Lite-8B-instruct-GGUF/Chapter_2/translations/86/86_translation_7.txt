 — Hurry up and bring some tea, Nastasya, because regarding tea, it seems one can do without the faculty. But here's the beer! — he moved to his chair, pushed the soup and beef towards him and began to eat with such appetite as if he hadn't eaten for three days. “I, brother Rodya, dine like this every day now,” he muttered, as much as his mouth full of beef would allow, “and it's all Pashenka, your little hostess, who is in charge, honoring me with all her heart. I certainly don't insist, well, I don't object either. And here comes Nastasya with the tea. She's so quick! Nastenka, do you want some beer?” “And go to the mischief!” “How about some tea?”
