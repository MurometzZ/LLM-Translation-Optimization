And he was opening the window onto the canal and peering out of it like a little pig squealing; and this is shameful. And how can one peer out of the window onto the street like a little pig squealing; and this is shameful. Fie upon it, fie upon it, fie! And Karl behind him was dragging him by his tailcoat from the window, and then, I swear to you, captain, he tore off his jacket. And then he shouted that he had to pay fifteen whole rubles in fines. And I myself, captain, paid five rubles for his torn jacket. And this is an impolite guest, captain, and he's causing a scandal! I'm saying, captain, you'll have big trouble with me because I can write about you in all the newspapers. — You're a writer, then? — Yes, captain, and what kind of impolite guest are you when you're in a noble house… — Oh no, oh no, oh no!
