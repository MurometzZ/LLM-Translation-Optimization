But instead, his head again leaned back on the pillow; once more, an unbearable chill gripped him; once more, he pulled on his overcoat. For a long time, several hours, he still had impulses, as if saying, "Now, without delay, go somewhere and throw everything away, to get rid of it once and for all, as soon as possible, as soon as possible!" He tried to get up from the couch several times, wanted to stand, but already couldn't. Finally, he was awakened by a strong knock on the door. "Open the door, alive or not? And all the time he's sleeping!" Nastasya shouted, pounding her fist on the door, "whole days, daytime, like a dog, he's sleeping!"
