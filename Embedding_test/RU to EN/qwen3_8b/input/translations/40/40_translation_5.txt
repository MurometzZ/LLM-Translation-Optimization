The impulse was so strong that he had already risen from his place to carry it out. "Should I think about it for a minute?"—ran through his mind. "No, better not to think at all and just get going!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Ilyi Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It's impossible to free both! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourselves: why call a servant if it's their affair? To report on themselves? Or for cunning? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen by both the servants and the townswoman at the very moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted from them at the very gates, asking about residence with his friends still by his side. Well, would he start asking about residence if he had such an intention?"
