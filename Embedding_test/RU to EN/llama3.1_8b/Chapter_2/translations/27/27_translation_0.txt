I've read it once or twice and I still don't understand. — What is this? — he asked the clerk. — This is a demand for payment of the loan with interest, collection. You must either pay all expenses, including interest and other charges, or give a written statement when you can pay, and at the same time an obligation not to leave the capital until payment and not to sell or hide your property. And the creditor has the right to sell your property and deal with you according to the law. — I don't owe anyone anything! — This is none of our business. We have received a protested loan note for 115 rubles, issued by you nine months ago to the widow Zarytsyna, a collegiate assessor, which was then transferred to payment to Counsellor Chubarov from the widow Zarytsyna, and we are inviting you to respond accordingly. — But she's my mistress!
