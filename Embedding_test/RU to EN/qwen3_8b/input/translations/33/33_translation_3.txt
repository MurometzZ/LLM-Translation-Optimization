There they are, the writers!—and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. Three days ago, there was an incident in the tavern: he had lunch but refused to pay; "I'll describe you in satire for that," he said. On the ship, too, there was another incident, last week: a respected family of a state councillor, his wife and daughter, were insulted with the most offensive words. Just yesterday, they threw out one of the customers from the confectionery. That's what they're like, the writers, literati, students, preachers… tsk! Go away! I'll come myself… then be careful! Did you hear? Louisa Ivanovna, with affected politeness, began to bow in all directions, and as she bowed, she managed to reach the door; but she bumped into a prominent officer at the door, with a fresh, open face and superb, thick, light blonde curls.
