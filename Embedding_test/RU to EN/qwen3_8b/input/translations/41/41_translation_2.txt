Well, will he inquire about residence if he came with such an intention? And Koch, that one, sat for half an hour below the silver tray and went upstairs to the old woman at seven forty-eight. Now figure it out…

— But allow me, how did such a contradiction arise: they themselves claim they knocked and the door was locked, but three minutes later, when the janitor arrived, it turned out the door was unlocked? — The trick is that the murderer was necessarily there, locked himself in, and would necessarily have been caught there if not for Koch's mistake, not going himself to the janitor. He precisely managed to descend the stairs during that interval and slipped past them somehow. Koch crosses himself with both hands: "If I had stayed there, he would have jumped out and killed me with an axe." Russian prayer wants to serve, he-he! — Nobody saw the murderer? — Where would you see that? The house is Noah's ark, — noted the clerk, who was listening from his place.
