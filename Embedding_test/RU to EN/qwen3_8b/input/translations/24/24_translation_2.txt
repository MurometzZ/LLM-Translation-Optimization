"Money? Which money? — thought Raskolnikov, — but... surely it's not that!" And he trembled with joy. Suddenly, it became to him extremely, immeasurably easy. Everything fell from his shoulders. — "When are you supposed to come, sir?" — shouted the poruchik, increasingly and more unknown in what way offended, — You are written to come at nine, but now it's already twelve o'clock! — "They brought me a quarter of an hour ago," — answered Raskolnikov loudly from behind, also suddenly and unexpectedly for himself getting angry and even finding some pleasure in it. — "That's enough that I came here sick with a fever." — "Don't shout!"
