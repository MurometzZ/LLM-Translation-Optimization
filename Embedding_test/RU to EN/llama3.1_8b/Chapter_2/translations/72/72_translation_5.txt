He looked at her barely breathing. — I myself heard... I didn't sleep... I sat, he said even more timidly. — I listened for a long time... The assistant warden came in... Everyone rushed to the staircase from all apartments...

— No one came. And it's screaming inside you with blood. It happens when there is no way out and it will start to roast your liver, then it starts to appear... You'll become something, right? He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, looking at him intently and not leaving. — Give me a drink... Nastasyushka. She went down and came back up with water in a white clay cup two minutes later; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He only remembered taking one gulp of cold water and spilling it from the cup onto his chest.
