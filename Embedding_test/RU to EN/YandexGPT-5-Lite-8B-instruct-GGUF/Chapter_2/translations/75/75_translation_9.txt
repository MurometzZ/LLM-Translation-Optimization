Having guessed that he had come to his senses, the landlady, peeping from the door, immediately closed it and hid. She was always shy and found it difficult to tolerate conversations and explanations; she was about forty years old, and she was stout and fat, dark-browed and black-eyed, kind from her corpulence and laziness; and she was even very pretty in appearance. Shy beyond necessity. “You… who are you?” he continued to question, turning to the foreman himself. But at that moment the door opened wide again, and Razumikhin entered, bending down a little because he was tall.”
