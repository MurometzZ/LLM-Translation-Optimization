It was strange. — Well, well, that's good, — concluded Ilya Petrovich, — we won't detain you. Raskolnikov left. He still managed to overhear a lively conversation that began upon his exit, in which Nikodim Fomich's questioning voice was most prominent… Out on the street, he completely came to himself. "Search, search, search right now! — he repeated to himself, hurrying to get out; — bandits! They suspect!" The same fear seized him from head to toe. II
