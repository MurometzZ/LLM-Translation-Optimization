— No need, — he said, — I came… here's the thing: I have no lessons… I wanted to… in any case, I don't need lessons at all… — Do you know what? You're raving! — remarked Razumikhin, who had been watching him intently. — No, I'm not raving… — Raskolnikov stood up from the sofa. As he rose to face Razumikhin, he didn't consider that he would have to confront him face to face. Now, in an instant, he realized, through experience, that he was least inclined to face anyone in the world at that moment. All his bile rose within him. He almost choked on his own fury at himself for having crossed the threshold into Razumikhin's presence. — Goodbye! — he suddenly said and went toward the door.
