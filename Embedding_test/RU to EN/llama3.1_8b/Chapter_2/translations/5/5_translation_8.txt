He repeated to himself, standing in the middle of the room, "Fragments of torn hem won't arouse any suspicions; it seems so, it seems so!" and with a painful attention began again to search around, on the floor and everywhere, if he hadn't forgotten anything else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple consideration left him, started to torment him unbearably. "What, is it really starting, is this really the execution approaching? Look, look, it's so!" Indeed, the scraps of braid which he had cut off from his pants were lying on the floor, in the middle of the room, for anyone to see first! "What's happening to me?" he cried out again like a lost man. Then a strange idea came into his head: maybe all his clothes are bloody, maybe there are many stains, but he doesn't see them, doesn't notice them because his consideration has weakened, is broken... his mind is clouded... Suddenly he remembered that the wallet was also bloody. "Ah! So it means that there must be blood in the pocket too, because I put a wet wallet into my pocket then!" He turned out the pocket at once and – yes, indeed – on the lining of the pocket there are stains, spots! "So it means I haven't lost all reason completely, so there is still consideration and memory, since I remembered myself and figured it out! – he thought with triumph, taking a deep breath all over his chest – just a fleeting weakness from fever, a momentary delirium," and he ripped off the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants.
