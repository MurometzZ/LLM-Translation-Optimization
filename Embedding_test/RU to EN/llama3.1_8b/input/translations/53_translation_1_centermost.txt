"Oh, what the devil is all this! — he thought suddenly in a fit of boundless spite. — Well, it's started, so let it start, the devil take her and her new life! How silly, Lord, how silly... And how much I've been babbling and flattering today! How disgusting I was fawning and flirting with that scoundrel Ilya Petrovich yesterday! But what's the point of all this? I don't care about them or about my own behavior. Not at all, not at all...

He suddenly stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question struck him like a blow and filled him with bitter amazement:

"If indeed everything was done consciously, not stupidly, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how did you manage to this day not even glance into your wallet and not know what you got, for which you took all the torments and deliberately went on such a vile, disgusting, low act?"
