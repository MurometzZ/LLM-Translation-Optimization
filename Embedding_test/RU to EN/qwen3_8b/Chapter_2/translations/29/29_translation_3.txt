— You, such a one and such a person! He suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the widow lady had already left), — what happened last night? a? Again, disgrace, making a scene for the whole street. Again, a fight and drunkenness. You're dreaming of peace! I've already told you, I've warned you ten times that I won't let you go at the eleventh hour! And you again, again, such a one you are! Even the paper fell from Raskolnikov's hands, and he stared wildly at the plump lady who was so rudely handled; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and immediately this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh… All his nerves were jumping. — Ilya Petrovich!
