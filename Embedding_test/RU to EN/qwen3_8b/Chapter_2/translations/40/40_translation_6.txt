The call was so strong that he already rose from his place to act. "Should I think for a minute?"—passed through his mind. "No, better not to think at all and just get going!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Ilyya Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It's impossible to free both of them! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourselves: why call a servant if it's their own business? To report on themselves? Or for cunning? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen right at the gates by both servants and the merchant's wife at the very moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted from them right at the gates, asking about residence with the servants, still with his friends. Well, would he start asking about residence if he came with such an intention?"
