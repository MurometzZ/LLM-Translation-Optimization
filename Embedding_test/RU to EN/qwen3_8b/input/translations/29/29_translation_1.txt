—You, such a peculiar and unconventional one," he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the widow had already left), "what happened last night? a? Another disgrace, causing a rowdy scene on the whole street. Another fight and drunkenness. You're dreaming of peace! I've already told you, I've warned you ten times that I won't let you go out at the eleventh hour! And you again, again, such a peculiar you, so unconventional! Even the paper fell from Raskolnikov's hands, and he stared wildly at the plump lady who was being treated so rudely; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and immediately this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping. —Ilya Petrovich!
