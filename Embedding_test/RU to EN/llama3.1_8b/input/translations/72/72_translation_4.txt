— No one came. And your blood is screaming in you. This happens when there's no way out and it will start to roast with the liver, then it starts to appear... You'll be something, right? He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, looking at him intently and not leaving. — Give me a drink... Nastasyushka. She went down and came back up two minutes later with water in a white clay cup; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He only remembered taking one gulp of cold water and spilling it from the cup onto his chest.
