And he was opening the window onto the canal and sticking his head out like a little pig squealing; and this is shameful. And how can one stick their head out of the window on the street like a little pig squealing; and this is shameful. Fie upon it, fie upon it, fie! And Karl behind him was pulling him by the tailcoat from the window, and then, I swear to you, captain, he tore his jacket. And then he shouted that he had to pay fifteen whole rubles in fines. And I myself, captain, paid five whole rubles for him. And this is an impolite guest, captain, and he's causing all sorts of trouble! I'm saying, captain, you'll get a big scolding from me because I can write about you in all the newspapers. — From writers, then? — Yes, captain, and what kind of impolite guest are you, captain, when you're in a noble house… — Ah-ah-ah!
