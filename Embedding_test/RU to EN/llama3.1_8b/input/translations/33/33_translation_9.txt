There they are, the writers! - and he cast a scornful glance at Raskolnikov. - There was an incident in the tavern three days ago: he dined, but refused to pay; "I'll write you up in satire for that," he said. On the steamer too, last week, he insulted with vile words a respectable family of a high-ranking official, his wife and daughter. From the confectionery yesterday they threw out one man for causing a commotion. That's what writers are like, literati, students, heralds... ugh! Off you go! I'll come to see you myself... then watch yourself! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna launched into a courteous bow in all directions and, as she bowed, reached the doors; but at the doors she bumped her rear end on one prominent officer, with an open fresh face and magnificent thick blond sideburns.
