There they are, the sociologists! — and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. Three days ago, there was an incident in the tavern: he had lunch, but refused to pay; "I'll describe you in satire for that," he said. On the ship, too, there was another incident, last week, when a respectable family of a state councilor, his wife and daughter, were insulted with the most offensive words. Yesterday, from the confectionery, I was thrown out for pushing. That's what they are, the writers, the literati, the students, the agitators… fiddlesticks! Go away! I'll come myself to you… then beware! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna, with an insincerely polite manner, began to sit down on all sides, and while sitting down, she managed to reach the doors; but she collided with an officer at the door, a man with an open, fresh face and with excellent, thick, blonde curls.
