 There they are, the writers! — and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. — The other day in the tavern there was also a story: had lunch, but doesn't want to pay; "I, say, will describe you in satire for that." On the boat too, another one, last week, the respectable family of a statsky советник, wife and daughter, called them the vilest words. The other day they kicked someone out of the pastry shop. That's what they are like, writers, literati, students, heralds... ugh! But you went! I myself will come to see you... then beware! Heard?
