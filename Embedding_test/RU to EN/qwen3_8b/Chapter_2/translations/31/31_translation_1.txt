And he opened the window from the ditch and stood in the window, like a little pig, squealing; and this is a disgrace. And how can one stand in the window to the street, like a little pig, squealing; and this is a disgrace. Fuh-fuh-fuh! And Karl pulled him from the window by the frock and here, indeed, sir captain, he tore his rock. And then he shouted that he had to pay fifteen shillings for the fine. And I myself, sir captain, paid five shillings for his rock. And this is an unworthy guest, sir captain, and causing every scandal! I, speaking, will be a big satirist, because I can write about you everything in all newspapers. — From writers, that is? — Yes, sir captain, and what kind of an unworthy guest, sir captain, when in a noble house… — Well, well, well!
