"If they ask, maybe I'll say," he thought, approaching the office. The office was a quarter of a verst away. She had just moved to a new apartment, a new building, to the fourth floor. He had once briefly visited her old apartment, but a long time ago. Upon entering the gates, he saw a staircase to the right, down which a man was coming with a book in his hands: "a janitor, meaning; meaning, that's where the office is," and he started going up at random. He didn't want to ask anyone anything. "I'll go in, kneel down, and tell everything..." he thought, entering the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep, and entirely filled with filth. All the kitchens of all apartments in all four floors opened onto this staircase and stood there almost all day. That's why there was a terrible stuffiness.
