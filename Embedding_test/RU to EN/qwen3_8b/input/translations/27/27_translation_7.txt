Read once, twice, and still didn't understand. — What is this? — asked he the scribe. — This is a demand for payment of a loan note, a recovery. You must either pay all costs, interest, and other charges, or give a written statement when you can pay, along with an undertaking not to leave the capital until payment is made and not to sell or conceal your property. And the lender is free to sell your property and act according to the law against you. — Well, I... owe no one! — This is no longer our concern. However, a overdue and legally protested loan note for one hundred fifteen rubles, issued to the widow Zarnitsynaya, collegiate assessor, nine months ago, and now transferred by the widow Zarnitsynaya to the town councilor Chebarov, has come to us for recovery, and therefore we are inviting you to respond. — But she is my housekeeper!
