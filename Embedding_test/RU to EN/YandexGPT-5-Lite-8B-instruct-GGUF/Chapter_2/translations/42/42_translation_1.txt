 "The house is like Noah's ark," remarked the clerk, listening from his place. "It's clear, it's clear!" Raskolnikov repeated fervently. "No, it's very unclear," Ilya Petrovich confirmed. Raskolnikov picked up his hat and went to the door, but he didn't reach the door...

When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him from the right, that another man was standing to his left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikodim Fomich was standing in front of him and staring at him; he got up from the chair. "What's going on, are you sick?" Nikodim Fomich asked rather sharply. "They could barely write with a pen even when they signed their names," the clerk noted, sitting down in his place and picking up papers again. "And how long have you been ill?" Ilya Petrovich shouted from his place, sorting through the papers as well.
