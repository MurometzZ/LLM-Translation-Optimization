His entire room was so small that you could hook a nail without getting out of bed. And indeed: the janitor and Nastasya are standing there. Nastasya somehow strangely looked him over. He gazed at the janitor with an impudent and desperate look. The latter silently handed him a grey, folded in half piece of paper sealed with a bottle seal. — It's a summons from the office, he said, handing him the paper. — From which office?... — To the police, that is, they're calling you to the office. You know what office it is. — To the police!
