— No need, — he said, — I came… here's the thing: I have no lessons… I wanted to… in any case, I don't need lessons at all… — Do you know what? You're talking nonsense! — remarked Razumikhin, who had been watching him intently. — No, I'm not raving… — Raskolnikov stood up from the sofa. As he rose to face Razumikhin, he didn't consider that he would have to meet him face to face. Now, in an instant, he realized, through experience, that he was least inclined to meet anyone face to face at this moment in the world. All his bitterness rose within him. He was almost overcome by hatred for himself the moment he stepped over Razumikhin's threshold. — Goodbye! — he said suddenly and went to the door.
