— No need, — he said, — I came… here's the thing: I have no lessons… I wanted to… in any case, I don't need lessons at all… — Do you know what? You're going mad! — remarked Razumikhin, who had been watching him intently. — No, I'm not raving… — Raskolnikov stood up from the sofa. As he rose to face Razumikhin, he didn't think about the fact that he was supposed to meet him face to face. Now, in an instant, he realized, through experience, that he was least inclined to meet anyone face to face at this moment in the whole world. All his bitterness rose within him. He almost choked on his own wrath at himself for having just crossed Razumikhin's threshold. — Goodbye! — he suddenly said and went towards the door.
