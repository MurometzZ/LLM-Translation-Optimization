 What is this? He rushed to take them out and throw them on the table in a flash. Having picked everything up, even turning his pockets inside out to make sure there was nothing else left, he carried all that heap into the corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place, the wallpaper had been torn away from the wall: immediately he began stuffing everything into this hole, under the paper: “It went in! Everything is out of sight, and so is the wallet!” — he thought joyfully, standing up and staring blankly into the corner, at the hole that was sticking out even more. Suddenly he shuddered all over with horror: “My God,” he whispered in despair, “what’s happening to me? Was this hidden? Is this how you hide things?”

Indeed, he had not expected any items; he thought there would be only money, that's why he hadn't prepared a place in advance — “but what am I happy about now? — he thought. — Is this how you hide things?”
