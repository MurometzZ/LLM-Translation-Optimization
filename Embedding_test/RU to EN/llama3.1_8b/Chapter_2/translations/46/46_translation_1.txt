Nothing and no one; nobody had looked in. Not even Nastasya had touched anything. But, good heavens! How could he have left all these things here yesterday? He rushed to the corner, thrust his hand under the wallpaper and began pulling out things and stuffing them into pockets. There were eight items altogether: two small boxes with earrings or something like that - he didn't take a close look; then four small leather cases. A chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else was also wrapped in newspaper, it seemed to be an order... He put everything into different pockets, into the coat and into the remaining right pocket of his trousers, trying to make it as unnoticeable as possible. He took a wallet along with the things too. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it wide open.
