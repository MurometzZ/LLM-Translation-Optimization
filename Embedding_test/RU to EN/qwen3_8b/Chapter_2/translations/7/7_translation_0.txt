Better throw it all out! — he kept repeating, sitting again on the couch — and now, at this very moment, without delay!.. But instead, his head again leaned back on the pillow; again a severe chill attacked him; again he pulled on his overcoat. For a long time, several hours, he still seemed driven by impulses to think, "Now, without delay, go somewhere and throw everything out, so that it's out of sight, as soon as possible, as soon as possible!" He tried to get up from the couch several times, wanted to stand up, but could no longer. Finally, he was awakened by a strong knock on the door. — Open the door, are you alive or not? And he's always sleeping! — Nastasya shouted, pounding her fist on the door — he's been sleeping all day, like a dog, all day long!
