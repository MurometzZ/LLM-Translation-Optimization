— No need, — he said, — I came… here's the thing: I have no lessons… I wanted to… in any case, I don't need lessons at all… — Do you know what? You're going mad! — remarked Razumikhin, who had been watching him intently. — No, I'm not raving… — Raskolnikov stood up from the sofa. As he rose to face Razumikhin, he didn't consider that he was about to meet him face to face. Now, in an instant, he realized, through experience, that he was least inclined, at this moment, to confront anyone in the whole world. All his bile rose within him. He almost choked on his own fury at himself for having crossed the threshold of Razumikhin's room. — Goodbye! — he suddenly said and went to the door.
