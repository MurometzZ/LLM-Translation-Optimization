The call was so strong that he had already risen from his place to carry it out. "Should I think for a minute?"—passed through his mind. "No, better not to think at all and throw it off his shoulders!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Ilyi Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It's impossible, both will be freed! First of all, everything contradicts; judge: why call a servant if it's their affair? To report on themselves? Or for cunning? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen by both the servants and the townswoman at the very moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted from them at the very gates and was inquiring about residence with the servants, still with his friends. Well, would he inquire about residence if he came with such an intention?"
