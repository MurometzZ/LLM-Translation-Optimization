Leave me alone! — Stay a moment, sycophant! You're completely mad! As for me, it's up to you. You see, I have no lessons, and I don't care, but there's the bookseller Херувимов on Толкучем, which is in itself a lesson. I won't exchange him for five merchant's lessons. He produces such publications and issues natural science books—how they sell! The titles alone are worth something! You always claimed I was foolish; by God, brother, there are people even more foolish than me! Now he's also getting into the direction; he feels nothing at all, well, and I, of course, encourage.
