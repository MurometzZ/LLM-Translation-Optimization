His entire room was so small that you could take off a hook without getting out of bed. So it is: the janitor and Nastasya are standing. Nastasya looked at him in a strange way. He looked at the janitor with a defiant and desperate expression. The janitor silently handed him a gray, folded-in-half paper sealed with a bottle-seal. "It's a summons from the office," he said, handing over the paper. "From which office?..." "They call the police, meaning to the office. It's known which office." "To the police!.."
