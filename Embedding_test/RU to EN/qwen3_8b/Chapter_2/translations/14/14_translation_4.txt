It was an ordinary notice from the quarter to appear today at half past ten in the office of the quarter's supervisor. "When did this ever happen? I have no business with the police on my own! And why exactly today?" he thought in agonizing confusion. "Lord, hurry up!" He was about to kneel down to pray, but even he laughed at himself— not at the prayer, but at himself. He hurriedly began to get dressed. "I'll be lost either way! Put on a sock!"
