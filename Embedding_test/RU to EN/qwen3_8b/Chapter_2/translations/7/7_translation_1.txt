There's not even a match. No, better to go somewhere and throw everything out. Yes! Better throw it all out! — he kept repeating, sitting again on the couch — and right now, this very minute, without delay!.. But instead, his head again leaned back on the pillow; again an unbearable chill seized him; again he pulled on his overcoat. For a long time, several hours, he still felt impulses to go somewhere, to throw everything out at once, without delay, so that it would be out of sight, as soon as possible, as soon as possible! He tried to get up from the couch several times, wanted to stand, but already couldn't. Finally, he was awakened by a strong knock on the door. — Open the door, alive or not? And he's always sleeping! — Nastasya shouted, pounding her fist on the door — whole days, daytime, like a dog, he's sleeping!
