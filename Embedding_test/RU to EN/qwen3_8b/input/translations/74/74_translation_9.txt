Then he would try to get up, wanting to run away, but someone would always stop him by force, and he would again fall into helplessness and confusion. Finally, he completely came to himself. This happened in the morning, at ten o'clock. At this hour in clear days, the sun always passed along his right wall in a long strip, illuminating the corner by the door. Standing by his bed was Nastasya, and another person, who was very curiously looking at him and completely unknown to him. He was a young man in a kaftan with a beard, and from his appearance, he looked like an artelshchik. The landlady peered out from the half-open door. Raskolnikov raised himself slightly. — Who is this, Nastasya? — he asked, pointing at the man.
