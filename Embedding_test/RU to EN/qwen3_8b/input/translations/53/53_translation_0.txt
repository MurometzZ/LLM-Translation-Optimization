"By God, this is it! — he suddenly thought, seized by inexhaustible wrath. — Well, it's begun, and so be it, let the devil take the new life! How foolish, Lord, how foolish!... How many lies I told and disgraced today! How vilely I flattered and played with the worst Ilyá Petrovich! But in any case, that's nonsense too! I don't care about them all, and I don't care about having flattered and played with them! It's completely different! It's completely different!..."

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question at once confused him and bitterly surprised him:

"If this whole matter was indeed done intentionally, not by accident, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how come you haven't even looked into your purse up to now and don't know what you've received, from which all these torments came, and why you intentionally went along with such a base, nasty, low act?"
