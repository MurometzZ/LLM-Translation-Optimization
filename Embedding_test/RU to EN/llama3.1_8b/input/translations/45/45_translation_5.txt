It was strange. — Well, all right, concluded Ilya Petrovich, we're not detaining you. Raskolnikov left. He could still hear the lively conversation that began as he exited, in which Nikodim Fyodorovich's questioning voice stood out most... On the street, he came to himself completely. "Search, search, now a search! — he repeated to himself, hurrying along; — bandits! they suspect!" His recent fear again enveloped him from head to toe. II
