Nothing and no one; no one had peeked. Not even Nastasya touched them. But, Lord! How could he leave all these things in this hole? He rushed to the corner, stuck his hand under the wallpaper, and started pulling out the items, loading them into his pockets. There were eight items in total: two small boxes with earrings or something similar—he hadn't looked closely; then four small sapphire cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper paper. Something else in the newspaper, perhaps an order… He put everything into different pockets, the coat, and the remaining right pocket of his pants, trying to make it inconspicuous. The wallet he also took along with the items. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it completely open.
