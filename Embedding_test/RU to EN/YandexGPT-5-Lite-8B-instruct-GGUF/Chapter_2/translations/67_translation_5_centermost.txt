 It seemed to him that at that moment he had cut himself off from everyone and everything, as if with scissors. He came back to his place only in the evening, which meant he had been out for about six hours. He did not remember anything about how and where he walked back. After undressing and shaking like a hunted horse, he lay down on the sofa, pulled his overcoat over himself, and immediately fell asleep...

He woke up in complete twilight from an awful scream. God, what a scream that was! He had never heard or seen such unnatural sounds, such howling, wailing, grinding, crying, beatings, and cursing. He could not even imagine such cruelty, such frenzy. In horror, he sat up on his bed, freezing and tormenting himself every moment. But the fights, screams, and curses grew stronger and stronger. And then, to his greatest изумление, he suddenly heard the voice of his landlady.
