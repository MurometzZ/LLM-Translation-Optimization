He pulled it off, but realizing there was nothing else, he took it and put it on again — and laughed again. "All of this is conditional, all is relative, all of this is just forms," — he thought briefly, with only a fragment of thought, while trembling all over, — for here he had put it on! For he had ended up by putting it on!" The laughter, however, immediately turned into despair. "No, it's not within my strength…" — he thought. His legs were trembling. "From fear," — he muttered to himself. His head was spinning and aching from the heat.
