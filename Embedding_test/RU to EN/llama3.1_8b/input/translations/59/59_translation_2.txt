Leave me alone! — Wait a minute, pipe-smoker! You're completely mad! As far as I'm concerned, you can do whatever you like. Look: I don't have any lessons, and what's it to me anyway? But there is this bookseller Hervimov on Tverskaya Street - that's a lesson in itself. I wouldn't trade him for five merchant's lessons. He publishes these little pamphlets and natural science books - and how they sell! Some of the titles are just priceless! You've always said I'm stupid; by God, brother, there are people stupider than me! And now he's even got a sense of direction; he doesn't know his own strength, but I, naturally, encourage him.
