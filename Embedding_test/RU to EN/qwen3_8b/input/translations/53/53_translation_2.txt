"By God, that's it! — he suddenly thought, seized by unrelenting fury. — Well, it's begun, so let it begin, by the devil with the new life! How foolish, Lord!... How much I lied and disgraced today! How disgusting I groveled and flirted with the most despicable Ilya Petrovich! Well, nonsense and this too! I don't care about them all, nor about the fact that I groveled and flirted! Completely different! Entirely different!..."

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question at once confused him and greatly astonished him:

"If indeed all this was done intentionally, not foolishly, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how come you haven't even looked into your wallet up to now and don't know what you've received, from which all these torments came, and why you deliberately went on such a base, disgusting, low deed?"
