There was a terrible stuffiness because of that. Up and down, janitors with books under their arms trudged, along with a motley crowd of people of both sexes—visitors. The door into the main office was also left ajar. He entered and stopped in the hallway. Here, everyone stood and waited for some men. Here, too, there was an extraordinary stuffiness, and additionally, the fresh, still wet paint on the old, rancid oil of the newly painted rooms was so nauseating it hit the nose. After waiting a bit, he decided to move forward into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low. Terrible impatience was pulling him further and further. No one noticed him.
