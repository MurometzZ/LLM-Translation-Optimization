"By God, that's it! — he suddenly thought, seized by inexhaustible wrath. — Well, here it begins, just as it should, let hell take the new life! How foolish, Lord!... How many lies I told and how thoroughly I disgraced myself today! How vilely I bowed and fawned over the most vile Ilyá Petrovich! But in any case, that's nonsense too! I care nothing for them all, nor for the fact that I bowed and fawned! Not at all! Not at all!..."

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question at once confused him and bitterly surprised him:

"If all this was indeed done deliberately, not foolishly, if you indeed had a definite and firm goal, then how come you have not even looked into your wallet up to now and do not know what you have received, from which all these sufferings came, and why you deliberately went on such a low, dirty, and despicable course?"
