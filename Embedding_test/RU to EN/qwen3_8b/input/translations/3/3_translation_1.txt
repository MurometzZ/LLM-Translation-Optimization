What is this? He immediately rushed to take them out and throw them on the table. Having gone through everything, even turning inside out his pockets to make sure nothing was left, he carried all this pile to the corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one spot, the wallpaper that had peeled away from the wall was torn; at once he began stuffing everything into this hole, under the paper: "It's in! Everything out of sight and out of mind, and the wallet too!" — he thought happily, standing up and staring blankly at the corner, the hole now even more gaping. Suddenly he shuddered all over with fear: "My God," he whispered in despair, "what has happened to me? Is this hidden? Is this how one hides?" Indeed, he hadn't expected anything but money; he thought there would be only money, and that's why he hadn't prepared a place in advance — "but now, now what am I happy about?" — he thought. "Is this how one hides?"
