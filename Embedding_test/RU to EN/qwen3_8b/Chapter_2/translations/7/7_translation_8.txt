Better to throw it all away!" he repeated, sitting back down on the couch, "and right now, this very minute, without delay!..." But instead, his head again leaned back on the pillow; again, an unbearable chill gripped him; again, he pulled on his overcoat. And for a long time, several hours, he still had impulses, as if saying, "Now, without delay, go somewhere and throw everything away, to get it over with, as soon as possible, as soon as possible!" He tried to get up from the couch several times, wanted to stand, but couldn't anymore. Finally, he was awakened by a strong knock on the door. "Open up, alive or not? And he's all sleeping!" cried Nastasya, pounding on the door with her fist, "All day long, like a dog, he's sleeping!"
