He remembered only how he had taken one gulp of cold water and spilled some from the cup on his chest. Then came a state of amnesia. III

However, he was not completely in a state of amnesia throughout his illness: it was an agitated condition with delirium and semi-consciousness. He later recalled much. It seemed to him that many people were gathering around him and wanted to take him away somewhere, arguing fiercely about him and quarreling. Suddenly he would be alone in the room, everyone having left and being afraid of him; they would only occasionally slightly open the door to look at him, threatening him, whispering something to each other, laughing and teasing him. Nastasya often came to mind beside him; he could distinguish another person as well, very much like someone familiar, but who exactly - he couldn't guess and longed for this, even crying. Sometimes it seemed to him that he had been lying there for a month; at other times - that the same day was passing. But about one thing or another - he completely forgot; however, every minute he remembered that he had forgotten something that could not be forgotten - he tormented himself, suffered, recalled, groaned, fell into fury or an unbearable, terrifying fear. Then he tried to get up from his place, wanted to run away, but always someone would stop him with force, and he would fall back into powerlessness and amnesia again.
