— But allow me, how could there be such a contradiction? They themselves claim they knocked and the door was locked, yet three minutes later, when they came with the janitor, it turned out the door was unlocked? — The trick is that the murderer was necessarily sitting there and locked himself in; he would surely have been caught there if it weren't for Koch's foolishness, if he hadn't gone himself to fetch the janitor. And precisely during that interval, he managed to descend the stairs and slip past them somehow. Koch crosses himself with both hands: "If I had stayed there," he says, "he would have jumped out and killed me with an axe." A Russian prayer wants to serve, he-he! — But no one saw the murderer? — Where could you see him? The house is like Noah's ark, — remarked the clerk, who was listening from his place.
