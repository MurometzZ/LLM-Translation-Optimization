He stared at her, scarcely breathing. — I heard it myself... I didn't sleep... I was sitting," he said, even more timidly. — I listened for a long time... The assistant of the overseer came... Everyone rushed down the stairs, from all the apartments... — No one came. But your blood is screaming. This is when she has no way out and her liver starts to burn, then hallucinations begin... Are you going to eat at all? He didn't answer. Nastasya remained standing over him, intently gazing at him and didn't leave. — Give me something to drink... Nastasyushka. She went downstairs and returned minutes later with water in a white clay cup; but he no longer remembered what came next. He remembered only taking a sip of cold water and spilling it from the cup onto his chest.
