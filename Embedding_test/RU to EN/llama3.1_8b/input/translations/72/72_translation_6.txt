He looked at her barely breathing. — I heard it myself... I didn't sleep... I sat, he said even more timidly. — I listened for a long time... The assistant warden came in... Everyone rushed to the stairs from all apartments...

— No one came. And there's blood crying out inside you. It happens when there's no way out and it starts to roast your liver, then it starts to appear... You'll be something, right? He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, staring at him intently and not leaving. — Give me a drink... Nastasya. She went down and came back up with water in a white clay cup two minutes later; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He only remembered taking one sip of cold water and spilling it from the cup onto his chest.
