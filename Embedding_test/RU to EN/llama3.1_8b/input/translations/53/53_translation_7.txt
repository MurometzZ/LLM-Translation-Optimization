"Oh, what the devil is all this! — he thought suddenly in a fit of boundless malice. — Well, it's started now, so let it start, to hell with her and her new life! How silly, Lord, how silly... And how much I've been flattering and fawning on them today! How disgusting I was and played the fool yesterday with that scoundrel Ilya Petrovich! But what's the point of all this? I don't care about any of them or even about my own foolishness. Not at all, not at all...!"

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question struck him like a blow and filled him with bitter amazement:

"If indeed everything was done consciously, not stupidly, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how did you manage to this day not even glance into your wallet and not know what you've got, for which you took all the torments and went deliberately into such a vile, disgusting, low thing?"
