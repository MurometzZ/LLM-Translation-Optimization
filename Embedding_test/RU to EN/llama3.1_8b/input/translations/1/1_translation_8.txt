"Third hour already!" he exclaimed, sitting down on the sofa - and suddenly everything came back to him! In that first moment, he thought he was going mad. A terrible chill gripped him; but it was also from the fever that had started in his sleep a long time ago. Now, suddenly, such a shiver struck him that his teeth almost fell out and everything inside him was trembling. He opened the door and began to listen: the whole house was fast asleep. With amazement, he looked around himself and everything in the room, not understanding how he could have done it yesterday, entering without locking the doors on a hook and throwing himself onto the sofa, not only undressed but even wearing his hat: it had slipped off and lay right there on the floor near the pillow. "If someone had come in, what would they think? That I'm drunk, but..." He rushed to the window.
