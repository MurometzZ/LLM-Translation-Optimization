Nothing and no one; no one had peeked. Even Nastasya didn't touch anything. But, Lord! How could he leave all these things in this hole? He dashed into the corner, stuck his hand under the wallpaper, and started pulling out items, loading them into his pockets. In total, there were eight items: two small boxes with earrings or something similar—he didn't look closely; then four small sapphire cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Another item in newspaper, seems like an order… He placed everything into different pockets, in the coat and the remaining right pocket of the pants, trying to make it inconspicuous. He also took the wallet along with the items. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it completely open.
