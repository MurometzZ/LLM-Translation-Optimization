"Oh, what the devil is all this! — he thought suddenly in a fit of boundless malice. — Well, it's started, so let it start, to hell with her and her new life! How silly it is, Lord... And how much I've been flattering and fawning on that wretched Il'ya Petrovich today! But what's the point anyway? I don't care about them all, or even about my own flattery and fawning. Not at all, not at all...!"

He suddenly stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question struck him like a blow and filled him with bitter amazement:

"If indeed everything was done consciously, not out of foolishness, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how did you manage to this day not even glance at your wallet and not know what's been given to you, for which you've taken all the trouble and deliberately gone down to such a vile, disgusting, low deed?"
