I will bring it. Raskolnikov looked at everything with deep amazement and a dull meaningless fear. He decided to remain silent and wait: what would happen next? "It seems I'm not in delirium," he thought, "it seems this is really...". Two minutes later Nastasya returned with soup and announced that now tea would also be served. To the soup came two spoons, two plates and all the tableware: saltshaker, pepper shaker, mustard for beef and other things that had not been seen in such an order for a long time before. The tablecloth was clean. "Not bad, Nastasya, if Praskovya Pavlovna ordered two bottles of beer. We'll drink it." "You're the one who's crazy," muttered Nastasya and went to carry out the order.
