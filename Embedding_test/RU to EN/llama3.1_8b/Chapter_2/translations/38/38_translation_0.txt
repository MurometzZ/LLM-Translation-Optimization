And where did these feelings come from? On the other hand, now, if suddenly the room were filled with his first friends, rather than quarterlies, then even then, it seems, he would not have a single human word for them, so suddenly empty had become his heart. A dismal feeling of painful and endless isolation and alienation suddenly became conscious to his soul. Not the baseness of his heart's outpourings before Ilya Petrovich, nor the poruchik's triumph over him turned his heart around so suddenly. Oh, what a matter now for him was his own baseness, all these ambitions, poruchiks, Germans, demands, offices and so on! If he were even sentenced to be burned at this moment, then he would not have stirred, and it is unlikely that he would have listened to the verdict attentively. Something was happening with him that was completely unfamiliar, new, sudden, and never before experienced. Not that he understood, but he clearly felt, with all the force of feeling, that not only could he no longer address these people in the quarter's office with his sensitive expansiveness as before, but even anything else; it would be pointless for him to turn to them, even if they were all his own brothers and sisters, rather than quarterlies, and there was no need for him to do so at any cost; he had never experienced a similar strange and terrifying feeling in his life. And what is most tormenting - this was more of an experience than a consciousness, more of a feeling than a concept; a direct and most tormenting feeling of all the sensations he had experienced in his life so far. The clerk began to dictate to him the form of a usual response in such cases, that is, I cannot pay, I promise then (sometime), I will not leave the city, I will not sell or give away property and so on.
