— Yes, Mr. Captain, and what an unhonorable guest, Mr. Captain, when in a noble home…  
— Well, well, well! Enough! I've already told you, told you, I've told you…  
— Ilya Petrovich! — the clerk spoke again significantly. The officer quickly glanced at him; the clerk slightly nodded his head. — …So here it is, esteemed Laviza Ivanovna, my last word, and this is the last time — the officer continued. — If there's another scandal in your noble home, I'll take you to the cugunder, as it's said in high style. Did you hear? So the writer, the author, took five groschen in "The Noble House" for a fold? There they are, the authors!
