"Oh, what the devil is all this! — he thought suddenly in a fit of boundless spite. — Well, it's started, so let it start, devil take her and her new life! How silly, Lord, how silly... And how much I've been babbling and flattering today! How disgusting I was fawning over that scoundrel Ilya Petrovich yesterday! But what's the point of all this? I don't care about them or anything else. It's not like that at all. Not like that at all..."

He suddenly stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question struck him off track and bitterly amazed him:

"If indeed everything was done consciously, not stupidly, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how did you manage to not even glance into your wallet and not know what you got, for which you've been suffering all this time and deliberately went on such a vile, disgusting, low deed?"
