— You, such and such a person!— he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the widow lady had already left),— what happened to you last night? Ah? Again disgrace, making a scene for the whole street. Again a fight and drunkenness. You're dreaming of peace! I've already told you, I've already warned you ten times that I won't let you go out at eleven! And you again, again, you such and such a person! Even the paper fell from Raskolnikov's hands, and he stared wildly at the plump lady, who was being treated so rudely; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and immediately this whole affair began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping.— Ilya Petrovich!
