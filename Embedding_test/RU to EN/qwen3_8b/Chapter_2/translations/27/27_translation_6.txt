Read once, twice, and didn't understand. — What is this? — asked he the scribe. — This is a demand for payment from you regarding the loan note, a collection. You must either pay all costs, interest, and other charges, or give a written response stating when you can pay, along with an undertaking not to leave the capital until payment is made and not to sell or conceal your property. The lender is entitled to sell your property and act according to the law. — Yes, I... owe no one! — This is no longer our concern. However, a overdue and legally protested loan note for 150 rubles, issued to you for the widow Zarnitsyna, collegiate assessor, nine months ago, which has now passed to the town councillor Chebarov for collection, and we are therefore inviting you to provide a response. — Yes, she is my housekeeper?
