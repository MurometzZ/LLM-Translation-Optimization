"What money? What kind of money?" thought Raskolnikov, "but... it must be something else!" And he shuddered with joy. It suddenly became unbearably easy for him. Everything had fallen off his shoulders. "And at what hour are you coming, sir?" the lieutenant shouted, becoming more and more offended by an unknown cause - they wrote to come at nine, but now it's already twelve o'clock! "They brought me a quarter of an hour ago," Rascolnikov replied loudly and over his shoulder, suddenly and unexpectedly for himself getting angry and even finding some pleasure in this. "And that's enough, since I came ill with fever." "Don't shout!"
