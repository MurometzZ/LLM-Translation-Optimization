"Don't bother," he said, "I came... well, I have no lessons to give... I was going to... but I don't need any lessons anyway."

"You know what?" Razumikhin observed, watching him closely. "You're just making excuses!" "No, I'm not making excuses..." Raskolnikov stood up from the sofa. As he got up and approached Razumikhin, he hadn't even thought about the fact that he would have to face him directly. Now, in an instant, he realized, on his own experience, that at this moment, he was least inclined to meet anyone face-to-face with anyone in the whole world. All his bile rose up in him. He almost choked from anger at himself for just stepping into Razumikhin's apartment. "Goodbye!" he said suddenly and headed towards the door.
