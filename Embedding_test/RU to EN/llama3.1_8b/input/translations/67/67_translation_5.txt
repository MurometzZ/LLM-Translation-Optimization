It seemed to him as if he had cut himself off from everyone and everything with scissors in this moment. He arrived back home already by evening, so it took him only six hours. Where and how he went back, he didn't remember anything. Undressed and shaking all over like a horse that has been beaten, he lay down on the sofa, put on his greatcoat and immediately fell asleep... He woke up in full darkness from an awful scream. God, what was this scream! Such unnatural sounds, such howling, wailing, screeching, sobbing, beating and cursing he had never heard or seen before. He couldn't even imagine such savagery, such frenzy to himself. In horror, he sat up on his bed, freezing every moment and suffering. But the fights, screams and curses were getting louder and louder. And then, to his greatest amazement, he suddenly caught a glimpse of his landlady's voice.
