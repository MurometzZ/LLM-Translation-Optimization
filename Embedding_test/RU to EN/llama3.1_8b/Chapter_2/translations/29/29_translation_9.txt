— And you, such-and-such and so-and-so, he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the mourning lady had already left), what happened to you last night? Ah? Again a scandal, you cause a commotion on the whole street. Again fights and drunkenness. You dream of being put in a straitjacket! For I've told you ten times already, for I've warned you already that I won't let you out at eleven! And again, and again, you're such-and-such and so-and-so! The paper even slipped from Razumikhin's hands, and he looked wildly at the plump lady who was being treated with such disrespect; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping like that. — Il'ya Petrovich!
