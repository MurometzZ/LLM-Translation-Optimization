"Rips of torn fabric won't arouse any suspicions; it seems so, it seems so!" he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with a painful attention began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere, had he forgotten something else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple reasoning left him, was starting to torment him unbearably. "What, is it really starting, is this really the execution arriving? Look, look, it's true!" Indeed, the oblongs of braid which he cut off from his pants were lying on the floor, in the middle of the room, so that anyone could see them first! "What's happening to me?" he cried out again like a lost man. Then a strange idea came into his head: what if all his clothes are bloody, what if there are many stains, but he just doesn't see them, doesn't notice them because his reasoning has weakened, is fragmented... his mind is clouded... Suddenly he remembered that the wallet was also bloody. "Ah! So it means that there must be blood in the pocket too, because I put a wet wallet into my pocket then!" He immediately turned out the pocket and - yes, indeed - on the lining of the pocket there are stains, spots! "So it means I haven't lost all reason completely, so there is still reasoning and memory, since I remembered myself and figured it out! - he thought with triumph, taking a deep breath with his whole chest, - just a weak moment of feverishness, a delirium for a minute," and he tore off the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants.
