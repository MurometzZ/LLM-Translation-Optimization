"Money? Which money? — thought Raskolnikov — but... surely, it's not that!" And he trembled with joy. Suddenly, it became to him extremely, immeasurably easy. Everything fell from his shoulders. — "When are you supposed to come, dear sir?" shouted the poruchik, growing more and more offended without knowing why — "You're supposed to come at nine, but now it's already twelve o'clock!" — "They brought me a quarter of an hour ago," answered Raskolnikov loudly from behind, also suddenly and unexpectedly for himself getting angry and even finding some pleasure in it. — "That's enough that I came here sick with a fever." — "Don't shout!"
