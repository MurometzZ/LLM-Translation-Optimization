"If they ask, maybe I'll say," he thought, approaching the office. The office was a quarter of a verst away. She had just moved to a new apartment, in a new building, to the fourth floor. He had been there briefly once before, but a long time ago. Upon entering the gates, he saw a staircase to the right, down which a man was coming with a book: "A janitor, that means; that means the office is here," and he started going up randomly. He didn't want to ask anyone anything. "I'll go in, kneel down, and tell everything..." he thought as he entered the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep, and entirely filled with filth. All the kitchens from all apartments in the four floors opened onto this staircase and stood there almost all day. That caused a terrible heat.
