"Oh, what the devil is all this! — he thought suddenly in a fit of boundless spite. — Well, it's started now, so let it start; may the devil take her and her new life! How silly it is, Lord... And how much I've been babbling and flattering today! How disgusting I was and flirting with that scoundrel Ilya Petrovich yesterday! But what's the point of all this? I don't care about them or about my own behavior. Not at all! Not at all!..."

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question struck him like a blow and bitterly amazed him:

"If indeed everything was done consciously, not stupidly, if you really had a specific and firm goal, then how did you manage to not even glance into your wallet and not know what you got, which is why you've been suffering all this time and deliberately went for such a vile, disgusting, low thing?"
