"Third hour already!" He sat on the sofa, and suddenly it all came back to him! In a single moment, everything came back to him! At first he thought he was going mad. A terrible chill gripped him; but the chill was also from the fever that had started in his sleep long ago. Now, suddenly, such a shiver struck him that his teeth almost fell out and everything inside him was trembling. He opened the door and began to listen: the house was completely asleep. With amazement he looked around himself and everything around in the room and couldn't understand: how could he have done it yesterday, entering, not locking the doors on a hook and throwing himself onto the sofa, not only not undressing but even wearing a hat: it had slipped off and lay right there on the floor near the pillow. "If someone had come in, what would they think? That I'm drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to look around himself from head to toe, at all his clothes: were there any signs?"
