And he was opening the window onto the canal and peering out of it like a little pig squealing; and this is shameful. And how can one peer out of the window onto the street like a little pig squealing; and this is shameful. Fie upon it, fie upon it, fie! And Karl behind him was dragging him by his tailcoat from the window, and then, I swear to you, captain, he tore off his epaulettes. And then he shouted that he had to pay fifteen silver marks as a fine, and I myself, captain, paid five silver marks for him. And this is an impolite guest, captain, and he's making a scandal! I'm saying, captain, you'll have big trouble with me because I can write about you in all the newspapers. — From writers, then? — Yes, captain, and what kind of impolite guest are you, captain, when you're in a noble house… — Ah-ah-ah!
