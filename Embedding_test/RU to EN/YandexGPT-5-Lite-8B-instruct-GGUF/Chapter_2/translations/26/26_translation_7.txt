 The clerk looked at them with a smile. The hot lieutenant was apparently puzzled. "This is none of your business!" he shouted finally, somewhat unnaturally loudly, "but please submit the review that you are required to provide. Show him, Alexander Grigorievich. There are complaints against you! You don't pay money! Look at this clear falcon flying out!" But Raskolnikov no longer listened and greedily grabbed the paper, searching for a quick solution. He read it once, twice, and did not understand.
