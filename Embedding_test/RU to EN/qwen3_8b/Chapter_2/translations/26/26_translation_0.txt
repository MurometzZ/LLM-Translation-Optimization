The clerk smiled at them. The hot-headed captain seemed visibly puzzled. "This isn't your business!" he shouted at last in an unnaturally loud voice, "Now, do please submit the review they require from you. Show him, Alexander Grigoryevich. Complaints about you! Don't pay money! What a clear eagle you've flown!" But Raskolnikov no longer listened and greedily grabbed the paper, seeking to quickly find the solution. He read it once, twice, and didn't understand.
