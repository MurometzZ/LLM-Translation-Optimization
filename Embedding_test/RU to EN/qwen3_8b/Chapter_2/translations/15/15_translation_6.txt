Put on the sock! – it suddenly occurred to him, – it will get even more dusty, and the traces will disappear. But just as he put it on, he immediately pulled it off with revulsion and horror. He pulled it off, but realizing there was nothing else, he took it and put it on again—and laughed again. "All of this is conditional, all is relative, all of this is just forms," – he thought briefly, with just a flicker of thought, while trembling all over, – for here he put it on! For he ended up by putting it on! The laughter, however, immediately turned into despair. "No, it’s not within my strength…" – it occurred to him. His legs were trembling. "From fear," – he muttered to himself. His head was spinning and aching from the heat.
