— But allow me, how did such a contradiction arise: they themselves claim they knocked and that the door was locked, yet three minutes later, when they came with the butler, it turned out the door was unlocked? — The trick is that the murderer was surely sitting there and locked himself in; he would surely have been caught there if not for Koch's carelessness, who didn't go himself to fetch the butler. And he precisely managed to descend the stairs during that interval and slip past them somehow. Koch crosses himself with both hands: "If I had stayed there, he would have jumped out and killed me with an axe." A Russian prayer service wants to be held, heh-heh! — But no one saw the murderer? — Where would you see him? The house is like Noah's Ark, — remarked the clerk, who was listening from his place.
