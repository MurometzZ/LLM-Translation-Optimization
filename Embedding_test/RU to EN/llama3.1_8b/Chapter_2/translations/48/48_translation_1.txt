But how would the cases not sink and float instead? And of course it's like that. Everyone will see. And as it is already, everyone looks at each other when they meet, glancing around as if their only concern was him. "Why so, or maybe I'm just imagining," he thought. Finally, an idea came to his head: why not go somewhere along the Neva? There are fewer people there, and it's less noticeable, and in any case more convenient, and most importantly - further from these places. And he was surprised suddenly: how did he spend whole half-hours wandering in despair and anxiety, and in dangerous places, when this couldn't have occurred to him before! And that's why he had wasted the whole half-hour on a reckless business, which had already been decided upon in his sleep, in a delirium. He became extremely absent-minded and forgetful and knew it.
