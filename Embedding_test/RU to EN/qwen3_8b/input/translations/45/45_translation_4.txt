It was strange. — Well, well, that's good, — concluded Ilya Petrovich, — we won't detain you. Raskolnikov left. He could still hear the lively conversation that began as he exited, with Nikodim Fomich's questioning voice being the most prominent… On the street, he completely came to himself. "Search, search, a search now! — he repeated to himself, hurrying to get there; — bandits! They suspect!" The old fear gripped him from head to toe. II
