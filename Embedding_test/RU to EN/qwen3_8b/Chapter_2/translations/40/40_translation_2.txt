The call was so strong that he already rose from his place to act. "Should I think for a minute?"—passed through his mind. "No, better not think at all and throw it off his shoulders!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Ilyi Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It's impossible to free both! First of all, everything contradicts; judge: why call a footman if it's their affair? To denounce themselves? Or for cunning? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen near the gates by both the footman and the townsman at the very moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted from them near the gates, asking about residence with the friends still present. Well, would he start asking about residence if he came with such an intention?"
