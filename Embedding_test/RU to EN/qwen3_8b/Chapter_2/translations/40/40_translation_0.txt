The impulse was so strong that he had already risen from his place to act. "Should I think for a minute?"—ran through his mind. "No, better not to think at all and throw it off his shoulders!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Ilyi Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It's impossible to free both! First of all, everything contradicts; judge: why call a footman if it's their affair? To report on themselves? Or for cunning? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen by both the footmen and the townswoman at the very moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted from them at the very gates, asking about residence with his friends still by his side. Well, would he start asking about residence if he came with such an intention?"
