When he went to university, it often happened to him, most often when returning home — perhaps a hundred times — to stop at exactly the same spot, gaze intently at this truly magnificent panorama and each time almost be surprised by his one unclear and unsolvable impression. An inexplicable chill always emanated from this magnificent panorama; it was filled with a mute and deaf spirit for him… He was astonished every time by his gloomy and mysterious impression and put off solving it, not trusting himself, to the future. Now he suddenly sharply remembered his previous questions and perplexities, and it seemed to him that he had not accidentally recalled them now. It already seemed to him strange and odd that he had stopped at the same place as before, as if he really imagined that he could think about the same things now as before, and be interested in the same old topics and pictures as he used to… so recently. Even it almost made him laugh, and at the same time his chest tightened painfully. In some depth, down there, somewhere barely visible under his feet, all his previous past, thoughts, tasks, themes, impressions, and this entire panorama, and himself, and everything, everything seemed to appear to him now.
