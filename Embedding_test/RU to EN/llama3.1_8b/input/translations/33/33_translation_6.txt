There they are, the writers! - and he cast a scornful glance at Raskolnikov. - There was a story about him in the tavern three days ago: he ate and didn't want to pay; "I'll write you into satire for that," he said. On the steamer too, another one, last week, a respectable family of a civil servant, his wife and daughter, he insulted with vile words. From the confectionery yesterday they threw out one man in a fit. That's what writers are like, literati, students, heralds... ugh! You go away! I'll come to you myself... then watch yourself! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna began to curtsy all around with a mockingly polite smile and, curtsying, reached the doors; but in the doorway she bumped her backside into one prominent officer, with an open fresh face and magnificent thick fair mustaches.
