"Three hours already!" He sat on the couch—and suddenly remembered everything! In an instant, he remembered everything! In the first moment, he thought he was going mad. A terrible cold enveloped him; but the cold was also from the fever, which had already started with him in his dream. Now, suddenly, such a chill struck that his teeth almost chattered and everything inside him trembled. He opened the door and began to listen: the house was completely asleep. He looked around himself and everything in the room in astonishment and didn't understand: how could he, upon entering yesterday, not lock the doors with the hook and jump onto the couch, not only without undressing but even with a hat on: it had slipped off and lay on the floor near the pillow. "If someone had come in, what would they think? That I'm drunk, but…" He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to examine himself, from head to toe, his entire attire: are there any signs?
