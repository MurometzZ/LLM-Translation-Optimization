 — Make haste with the tea, Nastasya, because regarding tea, it seems one can do it without the faculty. But here's some beer too! — he moved to his chair, pushed his soup and beef towards him and began to eat with such an appetite as if he hadn't eaten for three days. — I, brother Rodya, dine like this every day now with you, — he muttered as much as his mouth full of beef would allow, — and all this is Pashenka, your хозяйюшка, managing things, honoring me from the bottom of her heart. I разумеется, don't insist, well, I don't object either. And here comes Nastasya with tea. She's so nimble! Nastenka, do you want some beer? — Oh, go to the mischief-maker! — And some tea?
