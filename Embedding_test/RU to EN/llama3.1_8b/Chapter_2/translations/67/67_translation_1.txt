He felt as though he had somehow cut himself off from everything and everyone with scissors in that moment. He arrived back home by evening, so it took him only six hours. Where and how he got there, he didn't remember anything. Undressed and shaking all over like a horse that's been beaten, he lay down on the sofa, put on his greatcoat, and immediately fell asleep... He woke up in full darkness from an awful scream. God, what was that scream! Such unnatural sounds, such yelling, screaming, screeching, sobbing, beating, and cursing he had never heard or seen before. He couldn't even imagine such savagery, such frenzy to himself. In horror, he sat up on his bed, freezing in terror every moment. But the fighting, screams, and curses were getting louder and louder. And then, to his greatest amazement, he suddenly caught a glimpse of his landlady's voice.
