And he was opening the window onto the canal and peering out of it like a little pig squealing; and this is shameful. And how can one peer out of the window onto the street like a little pig squealing; and this is shameful. Fie upon it, fie upon it, fie! And Karl behind him was dragging him by his tailcoat from the window, and then, I swear to you, Captain, he tore off his tie. And then he shouted that he had to pay fifteen whole marks in fines. And I myself, Captain, paid five whole marks for his tie. And this is an impolite guest, Captain, and he's causing all sorts of commotion! I'm saying, Captain, you'll have a big headache from me because I can write about you in all the newspapers. — From writers, then? — Yes, Captain, and what kind of impolite guest are you, Captain, when you're in a noble house… — Ah-ah-ah!
