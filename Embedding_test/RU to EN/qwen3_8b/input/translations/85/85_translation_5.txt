Meanwhile, Razumikhin moved over to the sofa, awkwardly like a bear, wrapped his left hand around his head, despite the fact that he could have raised himself, and brought a spoonful of soup to his mouth with his right hand, blowing on it several times beforehand so he wouldn't burn. But the soup was only just warm. Raskolnikov greedily swallowed a spoonful, then another, the third. But as he was bringing several spoonfuls, Razumikhin paused and announced that regarding the further steps, they needed to consult with Zosimov. Nastasya entered, carrying two bottles of beer. — Want some tea? — I want. — Go ahead and make tea, Nastasya, because regarding tea, it seems one can do it without a faculty.
