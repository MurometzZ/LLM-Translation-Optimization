There weren't even any matches. No, it's better to get out somewhere and throw everything away. Yes! Better to throw it all away! — he repeated, sitting down on the sofa again, — right now, this very minute, no delay!.. But instead of that his head leaned back onto the pillow once more; an unbearable chill struck him again; he pulled his greatcoat over himself once more. And for a long time, several hours, it still seemed to him in fits and starts that "if only I could go somewhere right now, without putting it off, and throw everything away so that it would be gone from my eyes as soon as possible, as quickly as possible!" He tried to get up from the sofa several times, wanted to stand up, but couldn't. A strong knock at the door finally woke him up. — Open up, are you alive or not? And he's sleeping all day like a dog! — Nastasya shouted, knocking on the door with her fist, — whole daytime days, as if nothing was wrong!
