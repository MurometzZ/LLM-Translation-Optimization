What is this? He immediately rushed to take them out and throw them onto the table. Having taken everything, even rummaging through pockets to make sure there was nothing left, he carried all of it over to the corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place, the torn wallpaper had come loose from the wall: immediately he began stuffing everything into this hole under the paper: "it's gone! Everything is out of sight and even the wallet too!" - he thought joyfully, standing up and stupidly looking into the corner, where the hole was now gaping even more. Suddenly he shuddered with horror: "Good God," he whispered in despair, "what's happening to me? Is it really hidden? Do they hide things like this?"
