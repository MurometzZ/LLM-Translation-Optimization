And where did these feelings come from? On the other hand, now, if suddenly the room were filled not with quarterlies, but his closest friends, then even then, it seems, he would have found no human word for them, so suddenly empty had become his heart. A dismal feeling of painful and endless isolation and alienation suddenly became conscious to his soul. Not the baseness of his heart's outpourings before Ilya Petrovich, not the poruchik's triumph over him turned his heart around so suddenly. Oh, what a concern for himself now was all these vices, ambitions, poruchiks, Germans, demands, offices and so on! If he were even sentenced to be burned at this moment, then he would not have stirred, and it is unlikely that he would have listened to the verdict attentively. Something was happening with him that was completely unfamiliar, new, sudden, and never before experienced. Not that he understood, but he clearly felt, with all the force of feeling, that not only could he no longer address these people in the quarter's office with his sensitive expansiveness as before, but even anything else; it would be pointless for him to address them, even if they were all his own brothers and sisters, and not quarterlies, and never in any case; he had never experienced such a strange and terrifying feeling before. And what is most tormenting - this was more of an experience than a consciousness, than a concept; a direct experience, the most tormenting experience of all those he had experienced so far in life. The clerk began to dictate to him the form of the usual response in such cases, that is, I cannot pay, I promise then (sometime), I will not leave the city, I will not sell or give away property and so on.
