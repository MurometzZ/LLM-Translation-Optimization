He stared at her, scarcely breathing. "I heard it myself... I didn't sleep... I was sitting," he said even more timidly. "I listened for a long time... The deputy overseer came... Everyone ran down the stairs, from all the apartments..." "No one came. But your blood is crying out. This is when she has no way out and starts to burn her liver, then hallucinations begin... Are you going to eat at all?" He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, intently gazing at him and didn't leave. "Give me something to drink... Nastasyushka." She went downstairs and came back two minutes later with water in a white clay cup; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He remembered only how he took a sip of cold water and spilled it from the cup onto his chest.
