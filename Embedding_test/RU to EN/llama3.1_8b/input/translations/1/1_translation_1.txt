"Third hour already!" He sat on the sofa, and suddenly it all came back to him! In a single moment it all came back to him! At first he thought he was going mad. A terrible chill gripped him; but the chill was also from the fever that had started in his sleep long ago. Now suddenly such a shiver struck him that his teeth almost fell out and everything inside him was shaking. He opened the door and began to listen: the house was completely asleep. With amazement he looked around himself and all around the room, not understanding how he could have yesterday, entering, not locked the doors on the hook and thrown himself onto the sofa, not only not undressed but even in a hat: it had slipped off and lay right there on the floor near the pillow. "If someone came in, what would they think? That I'm drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to look himself over from head to toe, all his clothes: were there any signs?"
