There they are, the writers! - and he cast a scornful glance at Raskolnikov. - There was an incident in the tavern three days ago: he ate his meal but refused to pay; "I'll write you up in satire for it," he said. On the steamer too, last week, he insulted with vile words a respectable family of a high-ranking official, his wife and daughter. From the confectionery yesterday they threw out one man who was causing a commotion. That's what writers are like, literati, students, heralds... ugh! Off you go! I'll come to see you myself... then watch yourself! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna began to curtsy in all directions with a mockingly polite smile, and as she curtsied, she reached the doors; but at the doors she bumped into one prominent officer from behind, with an open fresh face and magnificent thick blond sideburns.
