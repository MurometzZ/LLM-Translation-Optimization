It was strange. — Well, all right, concluded Ilya Petrovich, we're not detaining you. Raskolnikov left. He could still hear the lively conversation that began as he exited, with Nikodim Fyodorovich's questioning voice standing out above the rest... On the street, he came to himself completely. "Search, search, now a search! — he repeated to himself, hurrying along; — bandits! they suspect!" The previous fear again enveloped him from head to toe. II
