And he opened the window of the ditch and stood in the window, like a little pig, squealing; and this is a disgrace. And how can one be in the window on the street, like a little pig, squealing; and this is a disgrace. Fufufufu! And Karl dragged him from behind by the frock from the window, and here, it's true, Captain, he tore the rock. And then he cried out that he had to pay a fifteen shilling fine. And I myself, Captain, paid him five shillings. And this is an unworthy guest, Captain, and making every scandal! I, saying, will be a big satire on you, because I can write everything about you in all the newspapers. — From writers, meaning? — Yes, Captain, and what kind of unworthy guest, Captain, when in a noble house… — Nununun!
