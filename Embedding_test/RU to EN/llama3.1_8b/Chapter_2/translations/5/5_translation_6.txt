He repeated to himself, standing in the middle of the room, "Fragments of torn hem won't arouse any suspicion; it seems so, it seems so!" and with a painful attention began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere, if he hadn't forgotten something else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple reasoning left him, started to torment him unbearably. "What, is it really starting, is this really the execution arriving? Look, look, it's so!" Indeed, the scraps of braid which he had cut off from his pants were lying on the floor, in the middle of the room, for anyone to see first! "What's happening to me?" - he cried out again like a lost man. Then a strange idea came into his head: maybe all his clothes are bloody, maybe there are many stains, but he just doesn't see them, doesn't notice them because his reasoning has weakened, is fragmented... his mind is clouded... Suddenly he remembered that the wallet was also bloody. "Ah! So then, it must be in the pocket too, because I put a wet wallet in my pocket then!" He instantly turned out the pocket and - yes, there were stains on the lining of the pocket! "So then, I haven't lost all reason completely, so there is still reasoning and memory, since I remembered myself and figured it out! - he thought with triumph, deeply and joyfully breathing in his whole chest - just a weak moment of feverishness, a delirium for a minute", and he ripped off the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants.
