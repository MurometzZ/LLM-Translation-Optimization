Yes, Captain, and what a dishonorable guest is this, Captain, when in a noble house...

— Ah-ah-ah! Enough! I've already told you, told you, I've already told you...

— Mr. Illya Petrovich! — the secretary said again with significance. The lieutenant quickly glanced at him; the secretary slightly nodded his head.

...So here it is to you, esteemed Lavisia Ivanovna, my last word, and this will be for the last time, — continued the lieutenant. — If there's even one more scandal in your noble house, I'll make you a cudgeon, as they say in high style. Did you hear? So a writer, a composer, five rubles for a fald in a "noble house"? Look at them, writers!
