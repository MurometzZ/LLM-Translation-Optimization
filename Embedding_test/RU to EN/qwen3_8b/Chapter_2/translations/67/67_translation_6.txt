It seemed to him as if he had cut himself off from everything with scissors at that moment. He came to himself by evening, meaning he had been unconscious for only six hours. He didn't remember where or how he had returned. Undressing and trembling all over like a driven horse, he lay on the sofa, pulled on his overcoat, and immediately fell asleep...

He awoke in pitch darkness from a terrible scream. God, what a scream! Such unnatural sounds, howling, screaming, screeching, weeping, brawling, and profanities he had never heard or seen before. He couldn't even imagine such cruelty, such frenzy. In terror, he raised himself up and sat on his bed, trembling and suffering every moment. But the fights, screams, and profanities grew stronger and stronger. And here, to his greatest amazement, he suddenly recognized his mistress's voice.
