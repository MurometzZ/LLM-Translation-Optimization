— Yes, Mr. Captain, and what an unworthy guest, Mr. Captain, when in a noble house…
— Well, well, well! Enough! I've already told you, told you, I've told you…
— Ilya Petrovich! — the clerk spoke again significantly. The officer quickly glanced at him; the clerk slightly nodded his head. — So here it is, esteemed Laviza Ivanovna, my last word, and this is the last time — the officer continued. — If there's another scandal in your noble house, I'll take you to the cugunder, as it's said in high style. Did you hear? So a literator, a writer, took five groschen in "The Noble House" for a fold? There they are, the writers!
