"Three hours already!" He sat on the couch—and suddenly everything came back to him! In an instant, he remembered everything! At first, he thought he was going mad. A terrible cold gripped him; but the cold was also from a fever that had already begun with him in his sleep. Now, however, a sudden chill struck him so strongly that his teeth almost came out, and everything inside him was trembling. He opened the door and began to listen: the whole house was completely asleep. He looked at himself and everything around him in the room with amazement and didn't understand: how could he, upon entering yesterday, not lock the doors with the hook and throw himself onto the couch, not only without undressing but even with his hat on? It had slipped down and lay on the floor, near the pillow. "If someone had come in, what would they have thought? That I'm drunk, but…" He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to examine himself, from head to toe, his entire outfit: are there any traces?
