Strange indeed. — Well, well, that's good, — concluded Ilya Petrovich, — we won't detain you. Raskolnikov left. He still managed to catch the lively conversation that began upon his exit, in which Nikodim Fomich's questioning voice was most prominent… On the street he completely came to himself. "Search, search, a search now! — he repeated to himself, hurrying to reach his destination; — bandits! They suspect!" The old fear seized him from head to toe. II

"What if there was already a search? What if I run into them right here?"
