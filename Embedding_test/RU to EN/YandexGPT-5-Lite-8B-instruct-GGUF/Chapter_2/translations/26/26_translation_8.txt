 The clerk looked at them with a smile. The hot lieutenant was apparently puzzled. "This is none of your business!" he shouted finally, somewhat unnaturally loudly, "but please submit the review that you are required to provide. Show him, Alexander Grigorievich. There are complaints against you! You don't pay money! Look what a clear falcon has flown out!" But Raskolnikov no longer listened and greedily grabbed the paper, searching for an answer as quickly as possible. He read it once, twice, and did not understand.
