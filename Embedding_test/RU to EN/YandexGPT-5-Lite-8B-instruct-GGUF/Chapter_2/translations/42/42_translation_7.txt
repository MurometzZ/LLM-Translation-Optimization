 "The house is like Noah's ark," observed the clerk, listening from his place. "It's clear, it's clear!" repeated Nikodim Fomich fervently. "No, it's very unclear," affirmed Ilya Petrovich. Raskolnikov picked up his hat and went to the door, but he didn't reach the door...

When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him from the right, that another man was standing to his left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikodim Fomich was standing in front of him and staring at him; he got up from the chair. "What's this, are you sick?" asked Nikodim Fomich rather sharply. "They could barely write with a pen even when they signed," observed the clerk, settling back into his place and resuming his papers. "And how long have you been ill?" shouted Ilya Petrovich from his place, also sorting through the papers.
