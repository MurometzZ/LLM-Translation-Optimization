— You, so such and such, — he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the widow lady had already left), — what happened last night? a? Again, disgrace, making a scene for the whole street. Again, a fight and drunkenness. You're dreaming of peace! I've already told you, I've warned you ten times that I won't let you go out at 11th! And you again, again, so such and such you are! Even Raskolnikov's paper fell from his hands, and he stared wildly at the wealthy lady who was being treated so rudely; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and immediately this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh… All his nerves were jumping. — Ilya Petrovich!
