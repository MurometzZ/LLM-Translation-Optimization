"By God, that's it! — he suddenly thought, seized by an unrelenting fury. — Well, here we go, here we go, may hell take the new life! How foolish, Lord, how foolish!... How many lies I told and how thoroughly I disgraced myself today! How vilely I flattered and played around with the most despicable Ilya Petrovich! Well, nonsense, even that! I don't care about them all, nor about the fact that I flattered and played around! Not at all! Not at all!..."

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question at once confused him and bitterly surprised him:

"If all this was indeed done deliberately, not by accident, if you indeed had a definite and firm goal, then how come you haven't even looked into your wallet up to now and don't know what you've received, for which you've endured all these torments, and for which you deliberately went down to such a base, wicked, and low deed?"
