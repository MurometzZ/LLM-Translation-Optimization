There they are, the writers!—and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. Three days ago, there was an incident in the tavern: he had lunch, but refused to pay; "I'll describe you in satire," he said. Another incident on the ship, last week, a respected family of a state councillor, his wife and daughter, were insulted with the most vulgar words. Yesterday, they threw me out of the confectionery for some reason. That's what writers, literati, students, and heralds are like… fiddlesticks! Go on, then! I'll come myself… then beware! Did you hear? Luzhina Ivanovna, with a falsely polite manner, began to bow in all directions, and while bowing, she managed to reach the doors; but she bumped into a prominent officer from behind, with an open, fresh face and thick, golden curls.
