The call was so strong that he had already risen from his seat to act on it. "Shouldn't I think for a minute?" a thought flashed through his head. "No, better not think at all and get rid of it!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking with passion to Il'ya Petrovich, and his words reached him:

— It's impossible that both will be released! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourselves: why would they call a janitor if it were their business? To inform on themselves, perhaps? Or out of cunning? No, it would be too cunning even! And finally, student Pestryakov was seen by the two janitors and the woman at the very gates at the exact moment when he entered: he walked with three friends and parted from them right at the gates and asked about living arrangements from the janitors while his friends were still there. So would he ask about living arrangements if he had such an intention?
