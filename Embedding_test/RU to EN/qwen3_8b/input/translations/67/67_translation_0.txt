It seemed to him that he had somehow cut himself off from everything and everyone at that moment with scissors. He came to himself by evening, meaning he had walked only six hours. He didn't remember where or how he had walked back. Undressing and trembling all over like a driven horse, he lay on the sofa, pulled on his overcoat, and immediately fell asleep...

He awoke in complete dusk from a terrible scream. God, what was that scream! Such unnatural sounds, such howling, screaming, screeching, weeping, abuse, and curses he had never heard or seen before. He couldn't even imagine such cruelty, such frenzy. In terror, he raised himself up and sat on his bed, each moment freezing and suffering. But the fighting, screams, and curses grew stronger and stronger. And here, to his greatest astonishment, he suddenly recognized his mistress's voice.
