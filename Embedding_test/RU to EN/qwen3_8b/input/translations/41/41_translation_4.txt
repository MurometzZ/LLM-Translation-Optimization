— But allow me, how did such a contradiction arise: they themselves claim they knocked and that the door was locked, but three minutes later, when they came with the doorman, it turned out the door was unlocked? — The trick is that the murderer was necessarily sitting there and locked himself in; and he would necessarily have been caught there if it weren't for Koch being careless and not going himself to fetch the doorman. And he exactly during that interval managed to descend the stairs and slip past them somehow. Koch crosses himself with both hands: "If I had stayed there, he would have jumped out and killed me with an axe." The Russian prayer wants to serve, he-he! — But no one saw the murderer? — Where would you see him? The house is like Noah's Ark, — remarked the clerk, who was listening from his place.
