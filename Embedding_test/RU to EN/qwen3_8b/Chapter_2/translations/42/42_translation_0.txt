"No, the matter is very unclear," Ilya Petrovich added. Raskolnikov lifted his hat and went toward the door, but he did not reach it... When he awoke, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him on the right, that another person was standing on the left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nicholas Fomich was standing before him, staring intently at him; he rose from the chair. "What is this, are you ill?" Nicholas Fomich rather sharply asked. "They signed barely with a pen," the scribe remarked, sitting down in his place and resuming his work with the papers. "How long have you been ill?" Ilya Petrovich shouted from his place, also rummaging through the papers.
