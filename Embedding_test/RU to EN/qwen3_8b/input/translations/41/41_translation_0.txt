Well, why would they start inquiring about residency if they came with that intention? As for Koch, he sat downstairs for half an hour before going up to the old woman, and exactly at seven forty-five he went up to her. Now, think about it...

— But allow me, how did such a contradiction arise? They themselves claim they knocked and the door was locked, yet three minutes later, when they came with the janitor, it turned out the door was unlocked? — The trick is that the murderer was definitely there, locked himself in, and would have been caught for sure if it weren't for Koch's foolishness, not going himself to fetch the janitor. He managed to descend the stairs during that time and slipped past them somehow. Koch crosses himself with both hands: "If I had stayed there, he would have jumped out and killed me with an axe." Russian prayer service wants to serve, he-he! — But no one saw the murderer? — Where would you see him? The house is Noah's ark, — noted the clerk, listening from his place.
