Realizing he had woken up, the owner, who had been peeking through the door, immediately closed it and hid. She had always been shy and endured conversations and explanations with reluctance; she was forty years old, plump and fat, with black eyebrows and black eyes, kind because of her plumpness and laziness; and she was even very smooth in her own way. She was shy beyond necessity. — You... who are you? — he continued interrogating, addressing the foreman directly. But at that moment the door opened wide, and, leaning slightly because he was tall, Razumikhin entered.
