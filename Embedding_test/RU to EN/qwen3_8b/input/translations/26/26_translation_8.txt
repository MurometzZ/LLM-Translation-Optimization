The clerk looked at them with a smile. The hot-headed captain was evidently puzzled. "This isn't your business!" he shouted finally in an unnaturally loud voice, "but you may as well submit the report they require from you. Show him, Alexander Grigoryevich. Complaints against you! Don't pay money! What a clear-sighted eagle you've flown!" But Raskolnikov no longer listened and eagerly grabbed the paper, seeking quick answers. He read it once, twice, and still didn't understand.
