There they are, the scribes!—and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov.—"Three days ago, there was an incident in the tavern: he had lunch but refused to pay; 'I'll describe you in satire for that,' he said. On the ship, too, another incident occurred last week: a respected family of a ministerial councillor, his wife and daughter, were insulted with the most vulgar words. Yesterday, from the confectionery, I was thrown out for some reason. So these are the scribes, writers, students, heralds… Ugh! Go away! I'll come myself… then beware! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna, with a falsely polite manner, began sinking into a chair on all sides and, sinking down, reached the doors; but she bumped into an officer's backside in the doorway, a man with an open, fresh face and thick, lustrous white curls."
