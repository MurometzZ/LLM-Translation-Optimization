And he opened the window from the ditch and stood in the window, squealing like a little pig; and this is a disgrace. And how can one stand in the window facing the street, squealing like a little pig; and this is a disgrace. Fufufufu! And Karl was pulling him by the frock from the window and here, indeed, Captain, he tore his coat. And then he shouted that he had to pay fifteen shillings as a fine. And I myself, Captain, paid him five shillings for the fine. And this is an ungentlemanly guest, Captain, and a troublemaker! I, speaking, will write a big satire on you, because I can write everything about you in all the newspapers. — From the writers, you mean? — Yes, Captain, and what kind of an ungentlemanly guest, Captain, when in a noble house… — Nunununu!
