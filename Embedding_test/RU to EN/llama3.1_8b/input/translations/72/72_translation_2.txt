He looked at her barely breathing. — I myself heard... I didn't sleep... I sat, he said even more timidly. — I listened for a long time... The assistant warden came... Everyone rushed to the staircase from all apartments...

— No one came. And it's screaming in you with blood. This is when there's no way out and she'll start to roast on your liver, then she'll start to appear... You'll be something, will you? He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, staring at him intently and not leaving. — Give me a drink... Nastasyushka. She went down and came back with water in a white clay cup two minutes later; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He only remembered taking one gulp of cold water and spilling it from the cup onto his chest.
