"Oh, what the devil is all this! — he thought suddenly in a fit of boundless spite. — Well, it's started, so let it start, to hell with her and her new life! How silly, Lord, how silly... And how much I've been flattering and fawning on them today! How disgusting I was and played the fool yesterday with that scoundrel Ilya Petrovich! But what's the point of all this? I don't care about any of them or what I did. Not at all! Not at all...

He suddenly stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question knocked him off balance and bitterly surprised him:

"If indeed everything was done consciously, not stupidly, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how on earth have you not even glanced into your wallet and don't know what's been given to you, because of which you've taken all the torments and deliberately went for such a vile, disgusting, low thing?"
