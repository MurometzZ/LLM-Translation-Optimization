 His head was dizzy and aching from the heat. "This is a trick! They want to lure me in with a trick and then knock me down at full speed," he continued to himself as he went out onto the stairs. "The bad thing is that I'm almost delirious... I might blurt out some stupidity..."

On the stairs, he remembered that he was leaving all his things like that, in the wallpaper hole — "and they probably arranged a search without him here," he recalled and stopped. But such despair and such, if I can say, cynicism of doom suddenly seized him that he waved his hand and went on. "If only it would be over soon!.."

Outside, the heat was unbearable again; there hadn't been a drop of rain in all those days.
