And where did these feelings come from? On the contrary, now, if suddenly the room were filled not with quarterlies, but his closest friends, then even then, it seems, he would have no human word for them, so suddenly empty had become his heart. A dismal feeling of painful, endless loneliness and alienation suddenly became conscious to his soul. Not the baseness of his heart's outpourings before Ilya Petrovich, not the poruchik's triumph over him turned his heart around so suddenly. Oh, what a matter now for him was his own baseness, all these ambitions, poruchiks, Germans, demands, offices and so on! If he were even sentenced to be burned at this moment, then he would not have stirred, and it is unlikely that he would have listened to the sentence attentively. Something was happening with him that was completely unfamiliar, new, sudden, and never before experienced. Not that he understood, but he clearly felt, with all the force of feeling, that not only could he no longer address these people in sensitive expansiveness, as before, but even anything else; it would be pointless for him to turn to them, even if they were his own brothers and sisters, and not quarterlies, poruchiks. And then there was no need for him to turn to them at all, or in any case of life; he had never experienced such a strange and terrifying feeling before. And what is most tormenting - it was more an experience than a consciousness, than a concept; a direct experience, the most tormenting experience of all those that he had lived through so far. The clerk began to dictate to him the form of an ordinary response in such cases, i.e., I cannot pay, I promise then (sometime), I will not leave the city, I will not sell or give away property and so on.
