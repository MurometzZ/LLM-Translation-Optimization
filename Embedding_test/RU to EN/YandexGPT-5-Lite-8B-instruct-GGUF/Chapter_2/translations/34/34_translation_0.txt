 Louise Ivanovna began curtsying in all directions with hurried любезностью and, while curtsying, backed up to the door; but at the door she bumped into a prominent officer from behind, with an open fresh face and excellent thick blond sideburns. It was Nikodim Fomich himself, the district supervisor. Louise Ivanovna поспешила to curtsy almost to the floor and flew out of the office with frequent small steps, bouncing.  
“Thunder again, thunder and lightning again, tornado, hurricane!” — Nikodim Fomich addressed Ilya Petrovich in a friendly and amiable manner, — “again they have stirred up my heart, again I’m boiling over! I heard it from the stairs.”  
“Yes what!” — said Ilya Petrovich with noble negligence (and even not “shto”, but somehow: “Da-a shta-a!”), moving to another table with some papers and картинно shrugging his shoulders with each step, wherever he steps, there goes the shoulder; — here you see: Mr. author, that is, a student, former, that is, doesn’t pay money, has given bills, doesn’t clear out the apartment, complaints are constantly filed against them, and you took offense when I lit a cigarette in their presence! They themselves commit fraud, but look at them here, please: this is their most attractive appearance now!  
“Poverty is not a vice, my friend, well, what of it!”
