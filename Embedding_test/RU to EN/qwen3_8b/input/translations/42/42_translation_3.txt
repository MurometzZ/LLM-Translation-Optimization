"No, the matter is very unclear," added Ilya Petrovich. Raskolnikov raised his hat and went toward the door, but he did not reach the door... 

When he awoke, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him on the right, that another person was standing on the left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nickodim Fomich was standing before him, looking intently at him; he rose from the chair. "What is this, are you ill?" Nickodim Fomich rather sharply asked. "They signed barely with a pen, as if barely writing," noted the note-taker, sitting down in his place and resuming his work with the papers. "How long have you been ill?" Ilya Petrovich shouted from his place, also sorting through the papers.
