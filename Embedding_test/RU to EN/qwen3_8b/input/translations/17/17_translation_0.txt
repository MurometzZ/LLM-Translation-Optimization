"If they ask, maybe I will say," he thought, approaching the office. The office was a quarter of a verst away from him. She had just moved to a new apartment, a new building, to the fourth floor. He had been there briefly once, but a long time ago. Entering under the gates, he saw a staircase to the right, down which a man was descending with a book in his hand: "A janitor, that means; that means the office is here," and he started going up randomly. He didn't want to ask anyone anything. "I'll go in, kneel down, and tell everything…" he thought as he entered the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep, and entirely filled with filth. All the kitchens of all the apartments in all four floors opened onto this staircase and stood like that almost all day. That's why there was a terrible stuffiness.
