"By God, that's it!—he suddenly thought, seized by an unrelenting fury. Well, it's begun, so it's begun, to hell with her and the new life! How foolish, Lord, how foolish!... And how many lies I told and disgraced myself today! How despicable I was, bowing and fawning on the most despicable Ilya Petrovich! And as for that, nonsense! I don't care about them all, nor about the fact that I bowed and fawned! It's entirely different! It's entirely different!..."

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question at once confused him and greatly astonished him:

"If indeed all this was done deliberately, not foolishly, if you indeed had a definite and firm goal, then how come you haven't even looked into your wallet yet and don't know what you've gotten, for which you've endured all these sufferings and deliberately went on such a low, dirty, despicable deed?"
