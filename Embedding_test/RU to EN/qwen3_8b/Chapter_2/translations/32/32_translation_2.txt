— Yes, Mr. Captain, and what an unworthy guest, Mr. Captain, when in a noble house…
— Well, well, well! Enough! I've already told you, I've already told you, I've told you…
— Ilya Petrovich! — the clerk spoke again significantly. The captain quickly glanced at him; the clerk slightly nodded his head. — …Here you go, esteemed Lady Laviza Ivanovna, my final word, and that's for the last time, — continued the captain. — If there's another scandal, even just one more time, in your noble house, I'll take you to the cugunder, as it's said in high style. Did you hear? So, a literary person, a writer, took five groschen in "The Noble House" for a fold? There they are, the writers!
