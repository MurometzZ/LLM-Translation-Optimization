— No need, — he said, — I came… here's the thing: I have no lessons… I wanted to… in any case, I don't need lessons at all… — Do you know what? You're talking nonsense! — remarked Razumikhin, observing him closely. — No, I'm not talking nonsense… — Raskolnikov got up from the sofa. As he rose to face Razumikhin, he didn't consider that he was about to meet him face to face. Now, in an instant, he realized, through experience, that he was least inclined to meet anyone face to face in the whole world at this moment. All his bile rose within him. He was almost choked by his own wrath at himself for having just crossed Razumikhin's threshold. — Goodbye! — he suddenly said and went to the door.
