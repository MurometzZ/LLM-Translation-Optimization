Guessing that he had woken up, the housekeeper, who had been peeking through the door, immediately closed it and hid. She had always been shy and reluctant to engage in conversations and explanations; she was forty years old, plump and fat, with black eyebrows and black eyes, kind from her plumpness and laziness; and she was very smooth in her manner. She was shy beyond necessity. — You… who are you? — he continued interrogating, addressing the foreman directly. But at that moment the door opened wide, and, slightly bending down because he was tall, Razumikhin entered.
