 No one noticed him. In the second room some scribes were sitting and writing, dressed perhaps a little better than he was, they looked like an odd sort of people. He addressed one of them. "What do you want?" He showed the summons from the office. "Are you a student?" the man asked, glancing at the summons. "Yes, a former student." The scribe glanced at him without any curiosity, though. It was some particularly disheveled person with a fixed idea in his gaze.
