— No need, — he said, — I came… here's the thing: I have no lessons… I wanted to… in any case, I don't need lessons at all… — Do you know what? You're talking nonsense! — remarked Razumikhin, who had been watching him closely. — No, I'm not talking nonsense… — Raskolnikov stood up from the sofa. As he rose to face Razumikhin, he didn't consider that he would have to confront him face to face. Now, in an instant, he realized, through experience, that he was least inclined to face anyone in the world at that moment. All his bile rose within him. He was almost choked by his own wrath at himself for having just crossed the threshold into Razumikhin's presence. — Goodbye! — he said suddenly and went to the door.
