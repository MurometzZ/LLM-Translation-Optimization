The clerk looked at them with a smile. The hot-headed lieutenant was evidently puzzled. "This isn't your business!" he shouted finally in an unnaturally loud voice, "so you may as well submit the review they require from you. Show him, Alexander Grigoryevich. Complaints about you! Don't pay money! What a bold eagle you've flown!" But Raskolnikov was no longer listening and greedily grabbed the paper, seeking to quickly find the solution. He read it once, twice, and still didn't understand.
