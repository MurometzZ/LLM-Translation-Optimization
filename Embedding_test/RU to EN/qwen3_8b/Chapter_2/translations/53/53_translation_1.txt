"By God, that's it! — he suddenly thought, seized by an unrelenting fury. — Well, it's begun, so it's begun, to hell with her and the new life! How foolish, Lord! How foolish!... And how much I lied and disgraced myself today! How despicable I was, bowing and fawning on the most despicable Ilyich Petrovich! And as for that, nonsense! I don't care about them all, nor about the fact that I bowed and fawned! It's entirely different! It's entirely different!..."

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question at once confused him and greatly astonished him:

"If indeed all this was done deliberately, not by accident, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how come you haven't even looked into your wallet yet and don't know what you've gotten, for what all the suffering you've endured and for such a low, dirty, despicable deed you've gone on purpose?"
