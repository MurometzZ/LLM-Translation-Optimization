Read once, twice, and didn't understand. — What is this? — asked he the scribe. — This is a demand for payment under the loan note, an enforcement. You must either pay all costs, interest, and other charges, or give a written response when you can pay, along with an obligation not to leave the capital city until payment is made and not to sell or hide your property. The lender is entitled to sell your property and act according to the law. — Yes, I... owe no one! — This is no longer our affair. However, we have received a overdue and legally protested loan note for 150 rubles, issued to you for the widow Zarnitsyna, collegiate assessor, nine months ago, which has now passed to the town councillor Chebarov for enforcement. Therefore, we are inviting you to provide a response. — Yes, she is my housekeeper!
