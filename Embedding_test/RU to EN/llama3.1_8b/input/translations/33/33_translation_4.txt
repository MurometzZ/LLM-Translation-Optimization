There they are, the writers! - and he cast a scornful glance at Raskolnikov. - There was an incident in the tavern three days ago too: he ate his meal but refused to pay; "I'll write you up in satire for it," he said. On the steamer last week, he insulted with vile words a respectable family of a civil servant, his wife and daughter. The confectionery threw out one of them just yesterday. That's what writers are like, literati, students, heralds... ugh! Off with you! I'll come to see you myself... then watch yourself! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna began to curtsy all around, curtsying and making her way towards the door; but in the doorway she bumped into a prominent officer from behind, with an open fresh face and magnificent thick blond sideburns.
