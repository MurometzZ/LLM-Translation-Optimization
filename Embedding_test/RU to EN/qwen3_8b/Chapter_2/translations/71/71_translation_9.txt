It became very unpleasant for him from this scrutiny, even frightening. "Nastasya, why are you silent?" he finally whispered weakly. "This is blood," she finally answered, quietly, as if speaking to herself. "Blood!... Which blood?..." he muttered, growing pale and stepping back against the wall. Nastasya continued to look at him silently. "No one beat the mistress," she said again in a strict and resolute voice. He looked at her, barely breathing.
