There's not even a match. No, better to go somewhere and throw everything out. Yes! Better throw it all out! — he repeated, sitting again on the sofa — and now, at this very moment, without delay!.. But instead, his head again leaned on the pillow; again a relentless chill seized him; again he pulled on his overcoat. And for a long time, several hours, he still kept feeling impulses, as if "now, without delay, go somewhere and throw everything out, so that it's out of sight, as soon as possible, as soon as possible!" He tried to get up from the sofa several times, wanted to stand up, but already couldn't. Finally, he was awakened by a strong knock on the door. — Open up, are you alive or not? And all the time he's sleeping! — cried Nastasya, pounding her fist on the door — all day long, like a dog, he's sleeping!
