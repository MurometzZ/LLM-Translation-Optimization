When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that a person was supporting him on his right, that another person was standing on his left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikonov was standing before him, staring intently at him; he rose from the chair. "What is this, are you ill?" asked Nikonov rather sharply. "They signed it so faintly, barely with a pen," noted the clerk, sitting down in his place and resuming his work on the papers. "How long have you been ill?" shouted Ilyich from his place, also going through the papers.
