But how will the cases not sink, but float? And of course it's like that. Everyone will see. And anyway, people already look at each other in this way when they meet, glancing around as if their only concern is him. "Why would it be so, or maybe I'm just imagining things," he thought. Finally, the idea came to him that it might be better to go somewhere along the Neva; there are fewer people, and it's less noticeable, and in any case more convenient, and most importantly - further away from these places. And he was surprised suddenly: how did he spend whole half-hours wandering in despair and anxiety, and in dangerous places, but this he couldn't have thought of before! And so he only wasted whole half-hours on a reckless business that had already been decided upon in his sleep, in a delirium! He became extremely absent-minded and forgetful and knew it.
