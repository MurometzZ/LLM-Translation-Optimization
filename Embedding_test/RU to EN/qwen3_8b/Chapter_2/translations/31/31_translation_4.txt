And he opened the window from the ditch and stood in the window, squealing like a little pig; and this is a disgrace. And how can one stand in the window to the street, squealing like a little pig; and this is a disgrace. Fuh-fuh-fuh! And Karl was pulling him by the coat from the window and there, indeed, sir captain, his noble rank was torn. And then he shouted that he had to pay fifteen shillings as a fine. And I myself, sir captain, paid him five shillings for the noble rank. And this is a base guest, sir captain, and a troublemaker! I, speaking, will be a big satirist, because I can write about you everything in all newspapers. — From writers, you mean? — Yes, sir captain, and what kind of a base guest, sir captain, when in a noble house… — Well, well, well!
