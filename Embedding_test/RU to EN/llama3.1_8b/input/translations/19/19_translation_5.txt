No one noticed him. In the second room sat and wrote some clerks, dressed a bit better than he was, but they looked like an odd bunch. He approached one of them. — What do you want? He showed him a summons from the office. — Are you a student? — asked that one, glancing at the summons. — Yes, a former student. The clerk glanced at him, however without any curiosity. It was some especially disheveled person with an immobile idea in his gaze.
