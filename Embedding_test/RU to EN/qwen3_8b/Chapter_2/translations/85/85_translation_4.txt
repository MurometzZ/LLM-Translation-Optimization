Meanwhile, Razumikhin moved to the sofa, awkwardly like a bear, wrapped his left hand around Raskolnikov's head, despite the fact that he himself could have lifted up, and brought a spoon of soup to his mouth with his right hand, blowing on it several times beforehand to prevent him from burning. But the soup was only just warm. Raskolnikov greedily swallowed one spoon, then another, the third. But after bringing several spoons, Razumikhin suddenly paused and announced that regarding further steps, they needed to consult with Zosimov.
