He thought to himself suddenly: "I'll put it on! It will get even more dusty and the tracks will disappear." But as soon as he put it on, he immediately tore it off with disgust and horror. He tore it off, but realizing there was no other option, he picked it up again and put it back on – and laughed again. "Everything is conditional, everything is relative, all of this are just forms," he thought briefly, by the edge of a thought, while trembling all over his body – after all, he had put it on! After all, that's what he ended with! His laughter was immediately replaced by despair. "No, not possible..." he thought to himself. His legs were shaking. "From fear," he muttered to himself. His head was spinning and aching from the heat.
