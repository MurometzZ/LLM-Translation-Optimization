"By God, that's it! — he suddenly thought, seized by an unrelenting fury. — Well, here we are, here we are, let hell take the new life! How foolish, Lord!... How much I lied and degraded myself today! How disgracefully I fawned and flattered the most despicable Ilyá Petrovich! Well, nonsense, even that! I don't care about them all, nor about the fact that I fawned and flattered! Not at all! Not at all!..."

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question at once confused him and greatly astonished him:

"If indeed all this was done intentionally, not carelessly, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how come you haven't even looked into your wallet so far and don't know what you've received, for what all the suffering was endured, and for such a vile, dirty, low act, intentionally undertaken?"
