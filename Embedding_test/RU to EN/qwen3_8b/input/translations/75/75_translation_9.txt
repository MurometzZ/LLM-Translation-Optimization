The mistress, who had been peeking through the door, realizing he had woken up, immediately closed the door and hid. She had always been shy and bore conversations and explanations with reluctance; she was forty, plump and fat, with black eyebrows and black eyes, kind because of her plumpness and laziness; and she was very smooth in her own way. Shy beyond necessity. — You… who are you? — he continued questioning, addressing the cooperator directly. But at that moment the door opened wide, and, leaning slightly because he was tall, Razumikhin entered.
