There they are, writers! - and he cast a scornful glance at Raskolnikov. - There was a story in the tavern three days ago too: he ate his meal but refused to pay; "I'll describe you in satire for that," he said. On the steamer as well, last week, a respectable family of a high-ranking official, his wife and daughter, were insulted with vile words. From the confectionery recently, one was thrown out in a fit. That's what writers are like, literati, students, heralds... ugh! You go ahead! I'll come to you myself... then watch yourself! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna launched into a courteous bowing on all sides and, as she bowed, reached the doors; but at the doors she bumped her backside against one prominent officer, with an open fresh face and magnificent thick blond sideburns.
