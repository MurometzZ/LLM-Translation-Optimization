It was strange. — Well, well, that's good, — concluded Ilya Petrovich, — we won't detain you. Raskolnikov left. He could still hear the lively conversation that began upon his exit, in which the questioning voice of Nikodim Fomich stood out most clearly… On the street, he completely came to himself. "Search, search, a search is coming! — he repeated to himself, hurrying to get there; — bandits! They suspect!" The old fear once again enveloped him from head to toe. II

"What if there was already a search? What if I just happen to catch them in my room?"
But here is his room. Nothing and no one; no one looked in.
