It was strange. — Well, well, that's good, — concluded Ilyá Petrovich, — we won't detain you. Raskolnikov left. He could still hear the lively conversation that began after his exit, in which the questioning voice of Nikodim Fomich was most prominent... On the street, he completely came to himself. "Search, search, a search is coming! — he repeated to himself, hurrying to get there; — bandits! They suspect!" The old fear gripped him from head to toe. II
