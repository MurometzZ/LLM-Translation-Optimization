 «What a stupid thing I have done», he thought to himself, «they have Sonya and I want her myself». But reflecting that it would be impossible to take it back now and that in any case he would not have taken her, he dismissed the thought with a wave of his hand and went back to his lodging. «Sonya also wants pomade», he said as he walked along the street, and he laughed maliciously — «such cleverness costs money.... Hm! And maybe Sonya herself will be bankrupt today, for there is always a risk, hunting big game... digging for gold... then they would all be without a crust tomorrow except for my money. Hurrah for Sonya! What a mine they have dug there!»
