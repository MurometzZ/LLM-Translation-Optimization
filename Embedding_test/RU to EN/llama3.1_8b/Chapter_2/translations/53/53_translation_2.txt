"Oh, what the devil is all this! — he thought suddenly in a fit of boundless spite. — Well, it's started, so let it start, to hell with her and her new life! How stupid it is, Lord... And how much I've been pouring out and flattering today! How disgusting I was fawning and flirting with that scoundrel Ilya Petrovich yesterday! But what's the point of all this? I don't care about them or what I did. Not at all! Not at all!.."

He suddenly stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question knocked him off balance and bitterly amazed him:

"If indeed everything was done consciously, not stupidly, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how on earth have you not even glanced into your wallet and don't know what's been given to you, because of which you've taken all the torments and deliberately gone for such a vile, disgusting, low thing?"
