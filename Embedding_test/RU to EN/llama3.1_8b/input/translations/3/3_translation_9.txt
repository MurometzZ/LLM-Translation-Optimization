What is this? He immediately threw himself at it and began to pull out and throw everything onto the table. Having taken out all of it, even rummaging through pockets to make sure nothing was left behind, he carried the whole pile over to the corner. There, in the very corner, on the bottom, in one place, the torn-off wallpaper from the wall had been ripped: immediately he began to stuff everything into this hole under a sheet of paper: "it's gone! Everything is out of sight and even the wallet too!" - he thought joyfully, standing up and stupidly staring at the corner, at the further enlarged hole. Suddenly he shuddered with horror: "Good God," he whispered in despair, "what's happening to me? Is it really hidden? Do they hide things like this?"
