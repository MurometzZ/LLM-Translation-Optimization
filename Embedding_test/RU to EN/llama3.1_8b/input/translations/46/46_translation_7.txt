Nothing and no one; nobody had looked in. Not even Nastasya had touched anything. But, Lord! How could he have left all these things here yesterday? He rushed to the corner, stuck his hand under the wallpaper and started pulling out things and stuffing them into pockets. There were eight items altogether: two small boxes with earrings or something like that - he didn't take a good look; then four small sapphire cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else was also wrapped in newspaper, it seemed to be an order... He put everything in different pockets, in the coat and in the remaining right pocket of his pants, trying to make it as unnoticeable as possible. The wallet was taken along with the things too. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it wide open.
