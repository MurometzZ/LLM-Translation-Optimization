Nastasya, in short, couldn't have noticed anything from afar, thank God! Then, trembling, he opened the summons and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was an ordinary summons from the district to appear today at half past ten in the district overseer's office. "When has this ever happened? I have no business with the police on my own! And why exactly today?" he thought in tormenting confusion. "Lord, just hurry up!" He was about to kneel down to pray, but even he laughed—not at the prayer, but at himself. He hurried to dress. "I'll be lost either way! Put on a sock!"
