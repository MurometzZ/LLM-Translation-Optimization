And he opened the window of the canal and stood in the window, like a little pig, squealing; and this is a disgrace. And how can one be in the window on the street, like a little pig, squealing; and this is a disgrace. Fufufufu! And Karl dragged him from behind by the coat from the window, and indeed, sir captain, he had a rock broken off. And then he shouted that he had to pay a fine of fifteen shillings. And I myself, sir captain, paid him five shillings as a fine. And this is an unworthy guest, sir captain, and making every scandal! I, saying, will be a big satirist on you, because I can write everything about you in all the newspapers. — From the writers, meaning? — Yes, sir captain, and what kind of an unworthy guest, sir captain, when in a noble house… — Well, well, well!
