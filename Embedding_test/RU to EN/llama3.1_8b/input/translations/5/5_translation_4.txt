"Rips of torn fabric won't arouse any suspicions; it seems so, it seems so!" he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with a painful attention began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere, if he had forgotten something else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple reasoning left him, was starting to torment him unbearably. "What, is it really starting, is this really the execution arriving? Look, look, it's so!" Indeed, the scraps of braid which he cut off from his pants were lying on the floor, in the middle of the room, for anyone to see first! "What's happening with me?" he cried out again like a lost person. Then a strange idea came to him: that maybe all his clothes are bloody, that there may be many stains, but he just doesn't see them, doesn't notice them because his reasoning has weakened, is fragmented... his mind is clouded... Suddenly he remembered that the wallet was also bloody. "Ah! So then it must be in the pocket too, because I put a wet wallet in my pocket then!" He immediately turned out the pocket and - yes, there are indeed stains on the lining of the pocket! "So then I haven't lost all reason completely, so there is still reasoning and memory, since I remembered myself and figured it out! - he thought with triumph, taking a deep breath with his whole chest, - just a weak moment of feverishness, a delirium for a minute," and he ripped off the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants.
