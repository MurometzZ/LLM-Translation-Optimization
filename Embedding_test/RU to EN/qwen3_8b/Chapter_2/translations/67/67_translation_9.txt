It seemed to him that he had somehow cut himself off from everything and everyone at that moment with scissors. He came to himself by evening, meaning he had walked only six hours. He didn't remember where or how he had walked back. Undressing and trembling all over like a driven horse, he lay on the sofa, pulled on his overcoat, and fell asleep immediately...

He awoke in pitch darkness from a terrible scream. God, what was that scream! He had never before heard or seen such unnatural sounds, such howling, wailing, screeching, tears, abuse, and curses. He couldn't imagine such cruelty or madness. In fear, he raised himself up and sat on his bed, trembling every moment and suffering. But the fights, screams, and curses grew stronger and stronger. And here, to his greatest amazement, he suddenly recognized his mistress's voice.
