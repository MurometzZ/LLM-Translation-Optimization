"What money? What kind of money?" thought Raskolnikov, "but... it must be something else!" And he shuddered with joy. It suddenly became unbearably easy for him. Everything had fallen off his shoulders. "And at what hour are you coming, sir?" the lieutenant shouted, becoming more and more outraged by the unknown - "You were written to come at nine, but now it's already twelve o'clock!" "I was given it just a quarter of an hour ago," Raskolnikov replied loudly over his shoulder, suddenly and unexpectedly getting angry and even finding some pleasure in this. "And that's enough, considering I came here ill with fever." "Don't shout!"
