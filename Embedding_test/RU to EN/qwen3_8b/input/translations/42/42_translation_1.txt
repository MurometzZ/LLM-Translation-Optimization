"No, the matter is very unclear," added Ilya Petrovich. Raskolnikov raised his hat and went toward the door, but he did not reach the door... When he awoke, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that a person was supporting him on his right, that another person was standing on his left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikolai Fomin was standing before him, staring intently at him; he stood up from the chair. "What is this, are you ill?" asked Nikolai Fomin rather sharply. "They signed it so faintly, barely with a pen," noted the clerk, sitting down in his place and resuming his work with the papers. "How long have you been ill?" shouted Ilya Petrovich from his place, also rummaging through the papers.
