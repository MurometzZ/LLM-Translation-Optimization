Nastasya, thank God, could not notice anything from afar! Then, trembling, he unsealed the notice and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was a regular notice from the quarter to appear today at half past ten in the office of the quarter's supervisor. "When did this ever happen? I have no dealings with the police on my own! And why exactly today?" he thought in agonizing confusion. "Lord, just hurry up!" He was about to kneel down to pray, but even he laughed at himself. He hurriedly started getting dressed. "I'll be gone anyway! Put on a sock!"
