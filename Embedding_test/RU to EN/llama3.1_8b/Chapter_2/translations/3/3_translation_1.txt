What is this? He immediately rushed to take them out and throw them onto the table. Having taken everything, even rummaging through pockets to make sure there was nothing left, he carried all of this heap to the corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place, the torn-off wallpaper from the wall had been ripped: immediately he began to stuff it all into this hole under the paper: "it's gone! Everything is out of sight and the wallet too!" - he thought joyfully, standing up and stupidly looking into the corner, into the even more protruding hole. Suddenly he shuddered with horror: "My God," he whispered in despair, "what's happening to me? Is it really hidden? Do they hide things like this?"
