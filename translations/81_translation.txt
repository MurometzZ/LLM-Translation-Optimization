 Уходи! 
The young man hastily walked away without saying a word. The inner door flew open wide, and curious faces were peering into the room. Coarse faces with pipes, cigarettes, and caps thrust themselves into the doorway while laughing. Further inside could be seen figures in dressing gowns thrown open, wearing costumes of indecent scantiness, some with cards in their hands. They were especially entertained when Marmeladov, dragged by his hair, shouted that it was a consolation for him.
