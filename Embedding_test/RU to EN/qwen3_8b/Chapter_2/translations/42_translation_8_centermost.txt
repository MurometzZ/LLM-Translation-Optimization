When he awoke, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him on the right, that another person was standing on the left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nicodim Fomich was standing before him, staring intently at him; he got up from the chair. "How long have you been ill?" cried Ilya Petrovich from his place, also going through the papers.
