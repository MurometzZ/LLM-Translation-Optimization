— Do they hide it like that? My mind is leaving me! In exhaustion he sat down on the sofa, and at once an unbearable chill shook him again. Mechanically, he pulled over his student winter coat, which was warm but already almost in tatters, lying next to him on a chair, covered himself with it, and sleep and delirium seized him all over again. He dozed off. No more than five minutes later, he leapt up again and at once, in a frenzy, rushed back to his clothes. "How could I fall asleep again when nothing has been done! It's true, it's true: the noose is still under my arm! I forgot, I forgot such a thing! Such evidence!" He pulled off the noose and quickly began to tear it into pieces, stuffing them under the pillow in his clothes. "Torn fabric scraps will not arouse any suspicion; it seems so, it seems so!" he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with an attention that was almost painful, began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere, if he hadn't forgotten anything else?
