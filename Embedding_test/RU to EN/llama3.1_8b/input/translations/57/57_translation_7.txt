"Don't bother," he said, "I came... well, I have no lessons to give... I was going to... but I don't need any lessons anyway." "You know what?" Razumikhin observed, watching him closely, "you're just making excuses!" "No, I'm not making excuses..." Raskolnikov stood up from the sofa. As he got up and approached Razumikhin, he hadn't even thought that he would have to face him directly. Now, in an instant, he realized, on his own experience, that he was least inclined at this moment to meet anyone face-to-face with anyone in the whole world. All his bile rose up in him. He almost choked from anger towards himself for having just stepped into Razumikhin's apartment. "Goodbye!" he said suddenly and headed for the door.
