The clerk smiled at them. The hot-headed captain seemed clearly puzzled. "This isn't your business!" he finally shouted in an unnatural loud voice, "but you may as well submit the report they require from you. Show him, Alexander Grigoryevich. Complaints about you! Don't pay money! What a bold eagle you've flown!" But Raskolnikov no longer listened and greedily grabbed the paper, seeking to quickly unravel the mystery. He read it once, twice, and still didn't understand.
