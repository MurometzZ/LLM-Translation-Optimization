Mechanically, he pulled up the old winter coat he had used as a student, warm though now almost in tatters, covered himself with it, and sleep and delirium once more seized him. He fell into oblivion. No more than five minutes later, he suddenly jumped up again and immediately, in a frenzy, rushed back to his dress. "How could I have fallen asleep again, with nothing done! He pulled the necktie and quickly began tearing it into pieces, stuffing them under the pillow in his underwear.
