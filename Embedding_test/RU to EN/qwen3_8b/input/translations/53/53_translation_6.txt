"By God, that's it! — he suddenly thought, seized by unrelenting fury. — Well, it's begun, and so be it, let the devil take the new life! How foolish, Lord, how foolish!... How much I lied and disgraced myself today! How vilely I bowed and fawned over the most vile Ilyich Petrovich! But in any case, that's nonsense too! I don't care about them all, nor about the fact that I bowed and fawned! It's completely different! It's entirely different!..."

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected, and extremely simple question at once confused him and bitterly surprised him:

"If this whole matter was indeed done intentionally, not like an idiot, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how come you haven't even looked into your wallet up to now and don't know what you've received, for which you've endured all these torments, and for which you deliberately went on such a low, dirty, and base course?"
