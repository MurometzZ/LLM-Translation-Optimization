Nastasya, could not have noticed anything from afar, thank God! He carefully unsealed the notice with trembling hands and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was a regular summons to appear today at half past nine in the quarter's office. "When did this ever happen before? I have no business with the police on my own! And why today? — he thought in agonizing confusion. 'Lord, hurry up!' He was about to kneel down to pray, but even he laughed at himself — not at the prayer, but at himself. He hurriedly started getting dressed. 'I'll be gone one way or another! Put on a sock!'
