When he awoke, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that a person was supporting him on his right, that another person was standing on his left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikodim Fomich was standing before him, staring intently at him; he got up from the chair. "What is this, are you ill?" Nikodim Fomich asked rather sharply. "They signed as if barely tracing with a pen," noted the clerk, sitting down in his place and resuming his work on the papers. "How long have you been ill?" cried Ilyia Petrovich from his place, also rummaging through the papers.
