It became very unpleasant for him from this gaze, even scary. — Nastasia, why are you silent? — he finally spoke timidly with a weak voice. — This is blood, — she finally answered, quietly, as if speaking to herself. — Blood!.. Which blood?.. — he murmured, paling and moving away from the wall. Nastasia continued to look at him silently. — No one beat the mistress, — she spoke again with a strict and resolute voice. He looked at her, barely breathing.
