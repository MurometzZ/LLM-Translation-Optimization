Then he tried to get up from his place, wanted to run away, but always someone would stop him by force, and he would fall into powerlessness and unconsciousness again. Finally, he completely regained consciousness. This happened in the morning at ten o'clock. At this hour of the morning, on clear days, the sun always passed through a long strip across his right wall and lit up the corner near the door. By his bedside stood Nastasya and another man who was very curiously looking at him and completely unknown to him. This was a young fellow in a caftan with a beard, and he looked like an artisan from afar. The landlady's head appeared from behind half-opened door. Raskolnikov sat up. — Who is this, Nastasya? he asked, pointing at the young man.
