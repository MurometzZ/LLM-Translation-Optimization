Then he would try to get up, wanting to run away, but someone would always stop him by force, and he would again fall into helplessness and confusion. Finally, he completely regained consciousness. This happened in the morning, at ten o'clock. At this time of the morning, on clear days, the sun always passed along his right wall, illuminating the corner by the door. By his bed stood Nastasya and another person, who was very curious in examining him and entirely unknown to him. This was a young man in a kaftan, with a beard, and from his appearance, he looked like an artel member. The landlady peeked through the half-open door. Raskolnikov raised himself. — Who is this, Nastasya? — he asked, pointing at the young man.
