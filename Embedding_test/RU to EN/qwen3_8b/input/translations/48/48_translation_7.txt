What if the cases don't sink but float? Yes, and of course that's the case. Everyone will see. And already, everyone looks at them as if their entire concern is about him. "Why should it be like that, or perhaps it's just me," he thought. Finally, it occurred to him that perhaps it would be better to go somewhere on the Neva. There are fewer people, it's less noticeable, and in any case more convenient, and above all, farther from these places. He suddenly realized: how could he have walked for half an hour in melancholy and anxiety, in dangerous places, and couldn't think of this before! And that's why he spent half an hour on a reckless thing, because it was already decided once in a dream, in a delirium! He became extremely distracted and forgetful and knew it.
