I've read it once or twice and I still don't understand. — What is this? — he asked the clerk. — This is a demand for repayment of the loan with interest, collection. You must either pay all expenses, including penalties and others, or give a written response when you can pay, and at the same time an obligation not to leave the capital until payment and not to sell or hide your property. And the creditor has the right to sell your property, and deal with you according to the law. — I owe no one anything! — This is none of our business. We have received a protested and overdue loan in the amount of 115 rubles, issued by you nine months ago to the widow Zarytsyna, collegiate assessor, which was then transferred for payment to Counsellor Chubarov, and we invite you to respond accordingly. — But she's my mistress!
