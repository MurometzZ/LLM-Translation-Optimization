What is this? He immediately threw himself at it and began to take everything out and throw it onto the table. Having taken everything, even rummaging through pockets to make sure there was nothing left, he carried all this heap to the corner. There, in the very corner, on the bottom, in one place, the torn wallpaper that had come away from the wall: immediately he began to stuff everything into this hole under the paper: "it's gone! Everything is out of sight and even the wallet too!" - he thought joyfully, standing up and stupidly looking at the corner, at the hole that had become even bigger. Suddenly he shuddered with horror: "Good God," he whispered in despair, "what's happening to me? Is it really hidden? Do they hide things like this?"
