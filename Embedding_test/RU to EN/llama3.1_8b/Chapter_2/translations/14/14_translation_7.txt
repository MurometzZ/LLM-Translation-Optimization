It was an ordinary summons from the district to appear today at half past ten in the office of the district supervisor. "When could this be? I don't have any dealings with the police on my own! And why just today?" he thought, bewildered. "God, let it be over quickly!" He had thrown himself onto his knees to pray, but even he laughed at himself - not at the prayer, but at himself. He hastily began to get dressed. "I'll lose everything anyway! Put on a sock!"
