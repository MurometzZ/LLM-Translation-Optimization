"Pieces of torn flannel will never raise suspicion; it seems so, it seems so!" he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with painful intensity began scanning around, on the floor and everywhere, wondering if he had forgotten something else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple reasoning had left him began to torment him unbearably. "What, is it really beginning, is it really the execution that is about to start? There, there, that's exactly it!" Indeed, the cuttings of the fringe he had cut from his pants were lying on the floor in the middle of the room, to be seen by the first person! "What is happening to me!" he exclaimed again, as if lost. A strange thought came to him: perhaps his entire outfit was in blood, perhaps there were many stains, but he just didn't see them, didn't notice because his reasoning had weakened, shattered... his mind was clouded... Suddenly he remembered that there was blood on his wallet. "Bah! So, that means there should also be blood in the pocket, since I had put the wet wallet into the pocket!" Immediately he turned the pocket inside out, and indeed, there were traces, stains on the lining! "So, he hasn't completely lost his mind, there is reasoning and memory, since he realized and guessed himself!" he thought triumphantly, deeply and joyfully inhaling with his whole chest, "it's just a feverish weakness, a momentary delirium," and he tore out the entire lining from the left pocket of his pants.
