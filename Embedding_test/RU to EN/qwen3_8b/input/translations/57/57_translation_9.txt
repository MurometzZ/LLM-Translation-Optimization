— No need, — he said, — I came… here's the thing: I have no lessons… I wanted to… in any case, I don't need lessons at all… — Do you know what? You're going mad! — remarked Razumikhin, who had been watching him intently. — No, I'm not raving… — Raskolnikov got up from the sofa. As he rose to face Razumikhin, he didn't think about the fact that he would have to confront him face to face. Now, in an instant, he realized, through experience, that he was least inclined to face anyone in the world at that moment. All his bitterness rose within him. He almost choked on his own fury at himself for having crossed the threshold of Razumikhin's room. — Goodbye! — he said suddenly and went to the door.
