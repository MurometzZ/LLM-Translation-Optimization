— No need, — he said, — I came… here's the thing: I have no lessons… I wanted to… in any case, I don't need lessons at all… — Do you know what? You're talking nonsense! — remarked Razumikhin, observing him intently. — No, I'm not… — Raskolnikov stood up from the sofa. As he rose to face Razumikhin, he didn't consider that he would have to meet him face to face. Now, in an instant, he realized, through experience, that he was least inclined, at this moment, to face anyone in the whole world. All his bile rose within him. He was almost choked by his own wrath at himself for having just crossed the threshold of Razumikhin's room. — Goodbye! — he said suddenly and went to the door.
