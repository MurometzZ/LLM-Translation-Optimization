 — And you, you such and such and this and that, — he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the mourning lady had already left), — what happened to you last night? huh? Shame again, making a racket in the street. Fighting and drinking again. You're dreaming of being put in a straitjacket! After all, I told you, I warned you ten times that I wouldn't tolerate it the eleventh time! And you again, again, you such and such! Even Raskolnikov's paper fell out of his hands, and he was staring wildly at the stout lady, who was being so unceremoniously handled; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and immediately this whole story began to please him very much. He listened with pleasure, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping. — Ilya Petrovich!
