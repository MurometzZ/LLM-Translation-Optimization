Read once, twice, and didn't understand. — What is this? — asked he the scribe. — This is a demand for payment under the loan note, an enforcement. You must either pay all costs, interests, and other charges, or give a written response when you can pay, and at the same time promise not to leave the capital until payment and not to sell or hide your property. The lender is free to sell your property and act according to the law. — Yes, I... owe no one! — This is no longer our affair. However, we have received a overdue and legally protested loan note for 150 rubles, issued to you to the widow Zarnitsyna, collegiate assessor, nine months ago, and now transferred by the widow Zarnitsyna to the town councilor Chebarov. We therefore invite you to provide a response. — Yes, she is my housekeeper?
