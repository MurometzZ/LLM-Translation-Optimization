We'll finish this and start translating about whales, then we'll mark some dull gossip from the second part of "Confessions" too; someone said that Khlebnikov was like Rousseau in his kind - a democrat, a revolutionary enlightener. I don't disagree, to hell with it! Do you want to translate the second sheet of "Is Woman Man?" If you do, take the text now, take your pen and paper - all this is official business - and take three rubles: since I took them ahead for the whole translation, for the first and second sheets, then three rubles will come straight to your share. And when you finish the sheet, you'll get another three kopecks.
