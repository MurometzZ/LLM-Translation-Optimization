But at this moment the door opened wide again, and, slightly leaning forward because he was tall, Razumikhin entered. — What a sea cabin! — he shouted as he entered, — I always hit my forehead; it's also called an apartment! And you, brother, have you come to your senses? I just heard from Pashenka. — I've just come to my senses, — said Nastasya. — I've just come to my senses, — the foreman nodded again with a smile. — And who are you, I ask you? — Razumikhin suddenly addressed him. — I am, as you can see, Vrazumikhin; not Razumikhin, as everyone calls me, but Vrazumikhin, a student, a nobleman's son, and he is my friend. Now, tell me, who are you?
