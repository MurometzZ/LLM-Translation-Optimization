There they are, the writers!—and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov.—"Three days ago, there was an incident in a tavern: he had lunch but refused to pay; 'I'll describe you in satire for that,' he said. On a ship, too, there was another incident, last week, when a respectable family of a state councillor, his wife and daughter, were insulted with the most offensive words. Just yesterday, from the confectionery, I was thrown out for some reason. So there you have them, writers, literati, students, heralds… ugh! Go away! I'll come myself… then be careful! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna, with an affected politeness, began to sit down on all sides and, while sitting down, managed to reach the doors; but she bumped into a prominent officer with an open, fresh face and thick, golden ringlets."
