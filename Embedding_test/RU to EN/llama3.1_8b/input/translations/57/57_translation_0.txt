"Don't bother," he said, "I came... well, I have no lessons to give... I was about to... anyway, I don't need any lessons at all."

"Do you know what?" Razumikhin observed, watching him closely. "You're rambling!" "No, I'm not rambling..." Raskolnikov stood up from the sofa. As he got up to Razumikhin, he didn't think about the fact that he would have to face him face-to-face. Now, in an instant, he realized, on his own experience, that he was least inclined at this moment to meet anyone face-to-face with anyone in the whole world. All his bile rose up in him. He almost choked from anger at himself for just stepping into Razumikhin's apartment. "Goodbye!" he said suddenly and headed towards the door.
