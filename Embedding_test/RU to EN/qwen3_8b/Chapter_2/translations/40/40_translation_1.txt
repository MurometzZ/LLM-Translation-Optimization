The call was so strong that he already rose from his place to act. "Should I think for a minute?"—ran through his mind. "No, better not to think at all and throw them off his shoulders!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Ilya Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It's impossible to free both! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourselves: why call a servant if it's their own business? To report on themselves? Or for cunning? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen by both the servants and the merchant's wife at the very moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted from them at the very gates, asking about residence with the friends still by his side. Well, would he start asking about residence if he came with such an intention?"
