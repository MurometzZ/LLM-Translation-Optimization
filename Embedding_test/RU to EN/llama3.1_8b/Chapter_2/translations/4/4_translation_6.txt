— Do they hide it like that? My mind is leaving me! In exhaustion he sat down on the sofa, and immediately an unbearable chill shook him again. Mechanically, he pulled over the winter coat that had been lying next to him on a chair, warm but already almost in tatters, covered himself with it, and sleep and delirium seized him once more. He dozed off. No more than five minutes later, he jumped up again and immediately, in a frenzy, rushed back to his clothes. "How could I fall asleep again when nothing has been done! It's true, it's true: the noose is still under my armpit! I forgot, forgot such a thing! Such evidence!" He pulled off the noose and quickly began to tear it into pieces, stuffing them under the pillow in his clothes. "Torn fabric scraps will not arouse any suspicion; it seems so, it seems so!" he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with intense pain attention began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere, had he forgotten anything else?
