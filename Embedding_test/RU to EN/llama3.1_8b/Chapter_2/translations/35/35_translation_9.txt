Poverty is not a vice, my friend, but indeed what can you do! It's well known that he couldn't bear an insult. You must have offended him somehow and not been able to restrain yourselves, continued Nikodim Fomich, addressing Raskolnikov with courtesy - but it was all in vain: the most blablablaring person, I'm telling you, a man, but powder, powder! He blew up, boiled over, burned out - and that's it! And everything passed! And as a result, only gold from his heart! They even nicknamed him "Lieutenant-Powder"...

— And what an army unit was that! — exclaimed Ilya Petrovich, very pleased that they had tickled him so pleasantly, but still half-asleep. Raskolnikov suddenly wanted to say something unusually pleasant to all of them. — Forgive me, Captain, he began quite freely, addressing Nikodim Fomich suddenly - take an interest in my situation... I'm even willing to ask them for forgiveness if I've somehow failed on my part.
