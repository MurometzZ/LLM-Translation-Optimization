"Money? What money? — Raskolnikov thought, — but... surely not that!" And he trembled with joy. Suddenly, it became to him extremely, immeasurably easy. Everything fell from his shoulders. — What time is written for you to come, my lord? — shouted the ensign, growing more and more offended without knowing why, — you are written to come at nine, but now it's already twelve o'clock! — They brought me only a quarter of an hour ago, — Raskolnikov answered loudly and over his shoulder, also suddenly and unexpectedly for himself angered and even finding some pleasure in it. — That's enough that I came here sick with a fever. — Don't shout!
