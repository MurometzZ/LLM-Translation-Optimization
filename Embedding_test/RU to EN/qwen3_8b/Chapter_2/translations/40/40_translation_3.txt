The impulse was so strong that he had already risen from his place to act. "Should I think for a moment?"—ran through his mind. "No, better not to think at all and just throw it off one's shoulders!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Ilye Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It's impossible to free both! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourselves: why call a watchman if it's their affair? To report on themselves? Or for cunning? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen by both the watchmen and the merchant's wife at the very moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted from them at the very gates, asking about residence with the watchmen, still with his friends. Well, would he start asking about residence if he came with such an intention?"
