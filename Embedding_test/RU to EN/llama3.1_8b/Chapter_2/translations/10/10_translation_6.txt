His entire room was so small that you could hook a nail without getting out of bed. And indeed: the janitor and Nastasya are standing there. Nastasya somehow strangely glanced at him over her shoulder. He looked at the janitor with an inviting and desperate expression. The janitor silently handed him a grey, folded twice paper sealed with bottle wax. — It's a summons from the office, he said, handing him the paper. — From which office?... — To the police, that is, they're calling you to the office. You know what office it is. — To the police!
