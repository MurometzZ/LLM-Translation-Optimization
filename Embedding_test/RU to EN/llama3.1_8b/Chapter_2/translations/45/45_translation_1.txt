It was strange. — Well, all right, concluded Ilya Petrovich, we're not going to detain you. Raskolnikov left. He could still hear the lively conversation that began as he exited, in which Nikodim Fyodorovich's questioning voice stood out most... On the street, he was completely awake again. "Search, search, now a search! — he repeated to himself, hurrying along; — bandits! they suspect!" His recent fear suddenly gripped him all over, from head to toe. II
