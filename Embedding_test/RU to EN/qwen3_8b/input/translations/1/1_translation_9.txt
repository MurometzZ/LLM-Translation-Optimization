"Three hours already!" He sat on the couch—and suddenly everything came back to him! All at once, in an instant, he remembered everything! At first, he thought he was going mad. A terrible cold gripped him; but the cold was also from the fever, which had already begun with him in his sleep. Now, however, a sudden chill struck him so strongly that his teeth almost came loose, and everything inside him was trembling. He opened the door and began to listen: everything in the house was completely asleep. He looked at himself in astonishment and everything around him in the room, not understanding how he could have entered yesterday without locking the doors and flung himself onto the couch, not only without undressing but even with his hat on; it had slipped down and immediately lay on the floor near the pillow. "What would someone have thought if they had come in? That I was drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to examine himself, from head to toe, his entire attire: were there any traces?
