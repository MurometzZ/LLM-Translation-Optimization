— Go quickly and get the tea, Nastasya, because, as for the tea, it seems one can do without the faculty. But here comes the beer! — he moved to his chair, brought the soup and meat closer, and started eating with such an appetite as if he hadn't eaten for three days. — I, brother Rodya, now have lunch here every day, — he muttered, as his full mouth of meat allowed, — and it's all Pashenka, your housekeeper, who's entertaining me wholeheartedly. I, of course, don't insist, nor do I protest. Here comes Nastasya with the tea. How quick she is! Nastenka, want some beer? — By the mischief! — And a tea?
