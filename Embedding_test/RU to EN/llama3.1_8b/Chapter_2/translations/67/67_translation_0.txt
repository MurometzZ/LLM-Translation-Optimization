He felt as if he had somehow cut himself off from everything and everyone with scissors at that moment. He arrived back home by evening, so it took him only six hours. Where and how he got there, he didn't remember anything. Undressing and shaking all over like a horse in distress, he lay down on the sofa, put on his greatcoat, and immediately fell asleep... He woke up to full darkness from an awful scream. God, what was that scream! Such unnatural sounds, such a howl, wail, screech, sobs, blows, and curses he had never heard or seen before. He couldn't even imagine such savagery, such frenzy. In horror, he sat up on his bed, freezing in terror every moment. But the screams, shouts, and curses grew louder and louder. And then, to his greatest amazement, he suddenly caught a glimpse of his landlady's voice.
