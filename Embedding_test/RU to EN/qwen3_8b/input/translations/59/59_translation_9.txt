Leave me alone! — Stay a minute, you pompous fellow! You're completely mad! As for me, whatever you say. You see, I have no lessons, and I don't care, but there's the bookseller Heruvimov on Tolkuchoy, which is in itself a lesson. I won't exchange him for five merchant lessons. He produces such publications and issues natural science books—how they sell! The titles alone are worth something! You always used to say I was foolish; by God, brother, there are people even more foolish than me! Now he's also gotten into the direction [of publishing]; he feels nothing at all, but of course, I encourage him.
