But how won't the cases sink and float instead? And of course it's like that. Everyone will see. And anyway, people already look at each other in this way when they meet, glancing around as if their only concern is him. "Why would it be so, or maybe I'm just imagining things," he thought. Finally, the idea came to him that it might be better to go somewhere along the Neva; there are fewer people, and it's less noticeable, and in any case more convenient, and most importantly - further from these places. And he was surprised suddenly: how could he have wandered for a whole half hour in despair and anxiety, and in dangerous places, when this hadn't even occurred to him before! And that's why he had wasted the entire half hour on such a reckless business, which had already been decided upon in his sleep, in a delirium. He became extremely absent-minded and forgetful and knew it.
