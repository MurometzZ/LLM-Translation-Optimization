And he was opening the window onto the canal and sticking his head out like a little pig squealing; and that's shameful. And how can one stick their head out of the window on the street like a little pig squealing; and that's shameful. Fie upon it, fie upon it, fie! And Karl behind him was pulling him by the tailcoat from the window, and then, this is true, Mr Captain, he tore his jacket. And then he shouted that he had to pay fifteen whole rubles as a fine, and I myself, Mr Captain, paid five whole rubles for him. And this is an impolite guest, Mr Captain, and making all sorts of scandal! I'm saying, Mr Captain, that you'll have a big headache from me because I can write about you in all the newspapers. — From writers, then? — Yes, Mr Captain, and what kind of impolite guest are you, Mr Captain, when in a noble house... — Ah-ah-ah!
