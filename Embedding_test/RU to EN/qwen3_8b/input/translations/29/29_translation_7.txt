— You, such a peculiar and unconventional one!— he suddenly shouted at the top of his lungs (the widow had already left),— what happened last night? Ah? Another disgrace, another rowdy scene for the whole street! Another fight and drunkenness. You're dreaming of peace! I've already told you, I've warned you ten times that I won't let you go out at the eleventh! And you again, again, you such a peculiar and unconventional one! Even the paper slipped from Raskolnikov's hands, and he stared wildly at the plump lady who was being so rudely treated; but soon, however, he realized what was going on, and immediately this whole affair began to please him very much. He listened with enjoyment, so much so that he wanted to laugh, laugh, laugh... All his nerves were jumping.— Ilya Petrovich!
