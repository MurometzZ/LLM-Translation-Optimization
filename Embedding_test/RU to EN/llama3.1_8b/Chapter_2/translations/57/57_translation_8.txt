"Don't bother," he said, "I came... well, I have no lessons to give... I was going to... but I don't need any lessons anyway." "You know what?" Razumikhin observed keenly as he watched him, "you're just making excuses!" "No, I'm not making excuses..." Raskolnikov stood up from the sofa. As he got up to Razumikhin, he didn't even think about the fact that he would have to face him directly. Now, in an instant, he realized, on his own experience, that he was least inclined at this moment to meet anyone face-to-face with anyone in the whole world. All his bile rose up in him. He almost choked from anger towards himself, just having crossed Razumikhin's threshold. "Goodbye!" he said suddenly and went towards the door.
