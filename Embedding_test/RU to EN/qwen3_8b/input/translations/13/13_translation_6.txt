Though he could scarcely think clearly at that moment, he felt that people would not treat him properly when they came to take him. "But... the police?" — Would you like some tea? Want some? I'll bring it; there's still some left... — No... I'll go: I'll go right now," he muttered, standing up. "Go, and you won't even descend the stairs?" — I'll go... — As you like. She went after the janitor. Immediately, he ran to the light to examine the sock and tassel: "There are stains, but not very noticeable; everything has gotten dirty, worn out, and already faded. Whoever doesn't know beforehand won't notice anything. Thank God, Nastasya couldn't have noticed anything from a distance!" Then, trembling, he opened the notice and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood.
