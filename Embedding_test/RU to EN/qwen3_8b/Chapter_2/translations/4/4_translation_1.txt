"—Is that how they hide it? My mind is truly leaving me!" Collapsing in exhaustion, he sat on the sofa, and immediately an unbearable chill shook him again. Mechanically, he pulled up the old student's winter coat lying nearby on the chair, warm but already almost in rags, covered himself with it, and once more sleep and delirium seized him. He fell into a trance. No more than five minutes later, he jumped up again, and immediately, in a frenzy, rushed back to his dress. "How could I have fallen asleep again, with nothing done! That's exactly it, that's exactly it: I still haven't taken off the necktie under my armpit! I forgot, I forgot about such a thing! Such a clue!" He pulled the necktie off and quickly began tearing it into pieces, stuffing them under the pillow in his underwear.
