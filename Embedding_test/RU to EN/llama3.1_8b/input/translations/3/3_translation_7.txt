What is this? He immediately threw himself at it and began to pull out and throw everything onto the table. Having taken out all of it, even rummaging through pockets to make sure nothing was left behind, he carried the whole pile over to the corner. There, in the very corner, on the bottom, in one place, the torn wallpaper that had come loose from the wall: immediately he began to stuff everything into this hole under the paper: "it's gone! Everything is out of sight and even the wallet too!" - he thought joyfully, standing up and stupidly looking at the corner, at the hole that was now even more gaping. Suddenly he shuddered with horror: "Good God, I whispered in despair, what's happening to me? Is it really hidden? Do they hide things like this?" 

Indeed, he hadn't counted on anything valuable; he thought there would only be money, so he hadn't prepared a place beforehand - "but now, now what am I happy about?" he thought. "Do they hide things like this?"
