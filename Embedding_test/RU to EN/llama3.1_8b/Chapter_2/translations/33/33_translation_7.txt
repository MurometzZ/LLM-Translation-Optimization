There they are, the writers! - and he cast a scornful glance at Raskolnikov. - There was a story about him in the tavern three days ago: he ate his meal but refused to pay; "I'll write you up in satire for it," he said. On the steamer too, last week, he insulted with vile words a respectable family of a government official, his wife and daughter. From the confectionery just yesterday they threw out one man for causing a commotion. That's what writers are like, literati, students, heralds... ugh! Off you go! I'll come to see you myself... then watch yourself! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna began to curtsy in all directions with a sly smile and, curtsying, reached the doors; but at the doors she bumped into one prominent officer from behind, with an open fresh face and magnificent thick blond sideburns.
