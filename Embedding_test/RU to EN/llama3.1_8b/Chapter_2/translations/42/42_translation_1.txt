The house is Noah's Ark, the clerk observed from his seat. "It's clear, it's clear!" Nikodim Fomich exclaimed heatedly. "No, it's very unclear," Ilya Petrovich countered. Raskolnikov raised his hat and headed towards the doors, but he didn't reach them... When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, being supported by someone on his right, with another person standing on his left holding a yellow cup filled with yellow water, and Nikodim Fomich standing in front of him staring at him intently; he stood up from the chair. "What's wrong, are you ill?" Nikodim Fomich asked rather sharply. "They signed it barely touching the pen," the clerk noted, sitting down on his seat again and resuming work with the papers. "And how long have you been ill?" Ilya Petrovich shouted from his seat, also rummaging through the papers.
