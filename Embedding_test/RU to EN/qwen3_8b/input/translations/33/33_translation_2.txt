There they are, the writers!—and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov. Three days ago, there was an incident in the tavern: he had lunch, but refused to pay; "I'll describe you in satire," he said. On the ship, too, there was another incident, last week: a respected family of a state councillor, his wife and daughter, were insulted with the most vulgar words. Yesterday, from the confectionery, I was thrown out for some reason. That's what they are, the writers, literati, students, heralds… fiddlesticks! Go on, then! I'll come myself… then beware! Did you hear? Luzhina Ivanovna, with an insincerely polite manner, began to sit down on all sides, and while sitting down, she managed to reach the doors; but she ran into an officer from behind at the door, a man with an open, fresh face and excellent thick golden mustaches.
