Finally, the idea came to him that it might be better to go somewhere along the Neva River instead. And he was surprised suddenly: how did he spend whole half an hour wandering around in despair and anxiety, and in dangerous places, when he couldn't even think of this before! And that's why he wasted the whole half an hour on a reckless business, which had already been decided upon in his sleep, in a state of delirium.
