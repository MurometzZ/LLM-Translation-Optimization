It was strange. — Well, well, that's good, — concluded Ilya Petrovich, — we won't detain you. Raskolnikov left. He still managed to overhear a lively conversation that began upon his exit, in which Nikodim Fomich's questioning voice was most prominent… Out on the street, he completely came to himself. "Search, search, search right now! — he repeated to himself, hurrying to get out; — bandits! They suspect!" The same fear that had gripped him from head to toe returned again. II

"What if there actually was a search? What if I run into them right here at home?"

But here is his room. Nothing and no one; nobody had peeked.
