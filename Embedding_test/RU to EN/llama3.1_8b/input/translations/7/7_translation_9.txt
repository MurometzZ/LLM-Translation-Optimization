There weren't even any matches. No, it's better to go somewhere and get rid of everything. Yes! Better to get rid of it all! — he repeated, sitting down on the sofa again, — right now, this very minute, no delay!.. But instead his head leaned back onto the pillow once more; an unbearable chill gripped him once more; he pulled his greatcoat over himself once more. And for a long time, several hours, it still seemed to him in fits and starts that "if only I could go somewhere right now, without putting it off, and get rid of everything so as to be rid of it all, quickly, quickly!" He tried to get up from the sofa several times, wanted to stand up, but couldn't. A strong knock at the door finally woke him up. — Open up, are you alive or not? And he's sleeping like a dog! — Nastasya shouted, knocking on the door with her fist, — day after day, just like a dog, sleeping all day!
