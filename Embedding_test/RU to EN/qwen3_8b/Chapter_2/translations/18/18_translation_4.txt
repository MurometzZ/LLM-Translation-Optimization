There was a terrible stuffiness because of that. Up and down, the janitors with books under their arms went up and down, strutting and various people of both sexes—visitors. The door to the very office was also left open. He entered and stopped in the hallway. Here, everyone stood and waited for some men. Here, too, there was an extraordinary stuffiness, and in addition, it hit the nose to the point of nausea with the fresh, still not dried paint on the rancid oil of the newly painted rooms. After waiting a bit, he decided to move forward into the next room. All the rooms were tiny and low. Terrible impatience pulled him further and further. Nobody noticed him.
