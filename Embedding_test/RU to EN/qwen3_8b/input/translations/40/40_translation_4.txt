The call was so strong that he was already on his feet to carry it out. "Should I think for a minute?"—ran through his mind. "No, better not to think at all and just throw caution to the winds!" But suddenly he froze as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking passionately to Ilyi Petrovich, and the words reached him: "It's impossible to free both! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourselves: why call a footman if it's their affair? To denounce themselves? Or for cunning? No, that would be too cunning! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen near the gates by both the footman and the townsman at the very moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted from them at the gates, asking about residence with them present. Well, would he start asking about residence if he came with such an intention?"
