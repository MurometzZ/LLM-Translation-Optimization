It seemed to him as if he had somehow cut himself off from everyone and everything with scissors in that moment. He arrived back home already by evening, so it took him only six hours. Where and how he went back, he didn't remember anything about it. Undressed and shaking all over like a horse that's been beaten, he lay down on the sofa, put on his greatcoat, and immediately fell asleep... He woke up in full darkness from an awful scream. God, what was that cry! Such unnatural sounds, such howling, wailing, screeching, sobbing, beating, and cursing he had never heard or seen before. He couldn't even imagine such savagery, such frenzy to himself. In horror, he sat up on his bed, freezing in terror every moment. But the screams, yells, and curses only grew louder and louder. And then, to his greatest amazement, he suddenly caught a glimpse of his landlady's voice.
