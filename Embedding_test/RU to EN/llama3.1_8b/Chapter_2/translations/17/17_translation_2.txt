If they ask me, I might say so," he thought, approaching the office. The office was a quarter of a verst from him. It had just moved to a new apartment, in a new house, on the fourth floor. On his old apartment he had been once briefly, but very long ago. Entering through the gates, he saw to the right a staircase, down which went a man with a book in his hands: "a janitor, so it's here that there is an office," and he began to climb up randomly. He didn't want to ask anyone anything about anything. "I'll go in, get on my knees and tell everything..." he thought, entering the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep and full of filth. All kitchens of all apartments in all four floors opened onto this staircase and stood like that almost the whole day. That's why there was a terrible stench.
