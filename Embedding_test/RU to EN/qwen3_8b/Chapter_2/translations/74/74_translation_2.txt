Then he tried to get up, wanted to run away, but someone always stopped him by force, and he would again fall into helplessness and confusion. Finally, he completely regained consciousness. This happened in the morning, at ten o'clock. At this hour in clear days, the sun always passed along his right wall in a long strip, illuminating the corner by the door. Standing by his bed was Nastasya and another person, who was looking at him with great curiosity and was completely unknown to him. He was a young man in a kaftan, with a beard, and from his appearance, he resembled an artelshchik. The landlady peered through the half-open door. Raskolnikov raised himself slightly. — Who is this, Nastasya? — he asked, pointing at the young man.
