"Three hours already!" He sat on the sofa — and then suddenly remembered everything! In the first moment, he thought he was going mad. A terrible cold gripped him; but the cold was also from the fever, which had already started with him in his sleep. Now, suddenly, such a chill struck him that his teeth almost came out, and everything inside him was trembling. He opened the door and began to listen: the house was completely asleep. He looked around himself and everything in the room in astonishment and didn't understand: how could he, entering yesterday, not lock the doors on the hook and throw himself onto the sofa, not only without undressing, but even in a hat: it had slipped and was lying on the floor, near the pillow. "What would someone have thought if they had come in? That I was drunk, but..." He rushed to the window. There was enough light, and he quickly began to examine himself, from head to toe, all his clothes: are there any traces?
