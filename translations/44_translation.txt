 ‘Semyon Zaharovitch is tired with his work at the office, he is resting, shh!’
They made me coffee before I went to work and boiled cream for me! They
began to get real cream for me, do you hear that? And how they managed
to save up money for a decent outfit—eleven rubles, fifty
copecks, I can't guess. Boots, cotton shirt fronts—most magnificent,
a uniform, they got ready all in splendid style, for eleven rubles and
a half. The first morning I came back from the office I found that Katerina
Ivanovna had cooked two courses for dinner—soup and salted meat with
horseradish—which we had never dreamed of until then.
