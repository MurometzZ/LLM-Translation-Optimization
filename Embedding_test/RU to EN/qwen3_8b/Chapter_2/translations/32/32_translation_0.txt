— Yes, Mr. Captain, and what an unworthy guest, Mr. Captain, when in a noble house…  
— Nun-nun-nun! Enough! I've already told you, told you, I've told you…  
— Ilyia Petrovich! — the scribe spoke again significantly. The lieutenant quickly looked at him; the scribe slightly nodded his head. — …Here you go, esteemed Laviza Ivanovna, my final word, and this is for the last time, — the lieutenant continued. — If there is another scandal in your noble house, even just once more, I'll take you to the very end, as is said in high style. Did you hear? So a writer, a storyteller, took five coins in the "noble house" for a fur? There they are, the storytellers!
