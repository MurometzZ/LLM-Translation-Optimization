Then he would try to get up, wanting to run away, but someone would always stop him by force, and he would again fall into helplessness and confusion. Finally, he completely regained consciousness. This happened in the morning, at ten o'clock. At this hour in clear days, the sun would always sweep across his right wall in a long strip, illuminating the corner by the door. Standing by his bed was Nastasya, and another person, who was examining him with great curiosity and was completely unknown to him. He was a young man in a kaftan, with a beard, and from his appearance, he looked like a cooperative member. The landlady peered out from the half-open door. Raskolnikov raised himself slightly. "Who is this, Nastasya?" he asked, pointing at the young man.
