— But allow me, how could there be such a contradiction? They themselves claim they knocked and that the door was locked, yet three minutes later, when they came with the doorman, it turned out the door was unlocked? — The trick is that the murderer was definitely sitting there and locked himself in; he would have been caught for sure if it weren't for Koch's foolishness, if he hadn't gone off on his own to fetch the doorman. And precisely during that interval, he managed to descend the stairs and slip past them somehow. Koch crosses himself with both hands: "If I had stayed there," he says, "he would have jumped out and killed me with an axe." The Russian prayer wants to serve, hehe... — But no one saw the murderer? — Where would you see him? The house is like Noah's Ark, — noted the clerk, who was listening from his place.
