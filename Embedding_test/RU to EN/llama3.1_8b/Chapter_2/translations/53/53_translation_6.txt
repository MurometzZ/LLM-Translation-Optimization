"Oh, what the devil is all this! — he thought suddenly in a fit of boundless spite. — Well, it's started now, so let it start, to hell with her and her new life! How silly, Lord, how silly... And how much I've been pouring out and flattering today! How disgusting I was fawning and flirting with that scoundrel Ilya Petrovich yesterday! But what's the point of all this? I don't care about them or about my own fawning and flirting. Not at all, not at all...

He suddenly stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question struck him like a blow and left him both puzzled and bitterly amazed:

"If indeed everything was done consciously, not out of foolishness, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how did you manage to this day not even glance into your wallet and not know what's been given to you, for which you've taken all the torments and deliberately gone down to such a vile, disgusting, lowly deed?"
