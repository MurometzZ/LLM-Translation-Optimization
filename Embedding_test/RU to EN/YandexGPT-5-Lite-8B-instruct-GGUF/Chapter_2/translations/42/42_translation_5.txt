 "The house is like Noah's ark," remarked the clerk, listening from his place. "It's clear, it's clear!" Raskolnikov repeated fervently. "No, it's very unclear," Ilya Petrovich confirmed. Raskolnikov picked up his hat and went to the door, but he didn't reach the door...

When he came to, he saw that he was sitting on a chair, that someone was supporting him from the right, that another person was standing to his left with a yellow glass filled with yellow water, and that Nikodim Fomich was standing in front of him and staring at him; he got up from the chair. "What's this, are you sick?" Nikodim Fomich asked rather sharply. "They could barely write with a pen even when they signed," the clerk noted, sitting down in his place and going back to work on the papers. "And how long have you been ill?" Ilya Petrovich shouted from his place, also sorting through the papers.
