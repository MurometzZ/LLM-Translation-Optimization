What's this? He immediately threw himself at it and started pulling everything out and throwing it onto the table. Having taken everything, even rummaging through pockets to make sure nothing was left behind, he carried all of this heap to the corner. There, in the very corner, on the bottom, in one place, the torn-off wallpaper from the wall had been ripped: immediately he began stuffing everything into this hole under the paper: "it's gone! Everything is out of sight and even the wallet too!" - he thought joyfully, standing up and stupidly looking at the corner, at the further enlarged hole. Suddenly he shuddered with horror: "My God," he whispered in despair, "what's happening to me? Is it really hidden? Do they hide things like this?" 

Indeed, he hadn't counted on objects; he thought there would only be money, so he hadn't prepared a place beforehand - "but now, what am I happy about? - he thought. - Do they hide things like this?
