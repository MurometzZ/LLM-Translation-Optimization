"Rips of torn fabric won't arouse any suspicions; it seems so, it seems so!" he repeated, standing in the middle of the room, and with a painful attention began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere, had he forgotten something else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple consideration left him, was starting to torment him unbearably. "What, is it really starting, is this really the execution arriving? Look, look, it's so!" Indeed, the scraps of braid which he cut off from his pants were lying on the floor, in the middle of the room, for anyone to see first! "What's happening to me?" he cried out again like a lost person. Then a strange idea came to him: that maybe all his clothes are bloody, that there may be many stains, but he just doesn't see them, doesn't notice them because his consideration has weakened, is fragmented... his mind is clouded... Suddenly he remembered that the wallet was also bloody. "Ah! So then, it must be in the pocket too, because I put a wet wallet in my pocket then!" He turned out his pocket at once and - yes, there are stains on the lining of the pocket! "So then, I haven't lost all reason completely, so there is still consideration and memory, since I remembered myself and figured it out! - he thought with triumph, taking a deep breath with his whole chest, - just a weak moment from fever, a delirium for a minute," and he ripped the lining out of the left pocket of his pants.
