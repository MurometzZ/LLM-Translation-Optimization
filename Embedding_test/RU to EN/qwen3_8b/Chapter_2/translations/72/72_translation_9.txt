He stared at her, scarcely breathing. — I heard it myself… I didn't sleep… I was sitting, — he said even more timidly. — I listened for a long time… The deputy of the overseer came… Everyone rushed to the stairs, from all the apartments… — No one came. But the blood in you is crying out. This is when she has no way out and her liver starts to cook, then hallucinations begin… Will you eat at all? He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, stared at him intently, and didn't leave. — Give me something to drink… Nastasyushka. She went downstairs and came back two minutes later with water in a white clay cup; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He remembered only how he took a sip of cold water and spilled it from the cup onto his chest.
