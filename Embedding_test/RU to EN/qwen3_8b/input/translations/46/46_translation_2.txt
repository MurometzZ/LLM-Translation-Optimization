Nothing and no one; no one had peeked. Even Nastasya hadn't touched anything. But, Lord! How could he leave all these things in this hole? He rushed to the corner, stuck his hand under the wallpaper, and started pulling out items, loading them into his pockets. There were eight items in total: two small boxes with earrings or something like that—he hadn't looked closely; then four small sapphire cases. One chain was simply wrapped in newspaper. Something else in newspaper, seems like an order… He put everything into different pockets, the coat, and the remaining right pants pocket, trying to make it less noticeable. The wallet also took along with the items. Then he left the room, this time even leaving it completely open.
