He repeated to himself, standing in the middle of the room, "Pieces of torn fabric won't arouse any suspicions; it seems so, it seems so!" and with a painful attention began to search around again, on the floor and everywhere, if he had forgotten something else? The certainty that everything, even memory, even simple reasoning, left him was starting to torment him unbearably. "Is it really starting, is this really the execution?" he exclaimed, pointing at the oblongs of braid that he had cut off from his pants lying on the floor in the middle of the room for anyone to see! "What's happening to me!" he cried out again like a lost man. Then a strange idea came to him: maybe all his clothes are bloody, maybe there are many stains, but he doesn't see them, doesn't notice them because his reasoning has weakened, is fragmented... his mind is clouded... Suddenly he remembered that the wallet was also bloody. "Ah! So it means there must be blood in the pocket too, because I put a wet wallet in my pocket then!" He immediately turned out the pocket and - yes, there were stains on the lining of the pocket! "So it means I haven't lost all reason completely, so there is still reasoning and memory, since I remembered myself and figured it out!" he thought triumphantly, taking a deep breath with his whole chest. "Just a fleeting weakness from fever, a momentary delirium," and he ripped off the lining of the left pocket of his pants.
