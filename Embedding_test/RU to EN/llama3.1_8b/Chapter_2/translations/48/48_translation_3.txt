But how will the cases not sink and float? And of course they will. Everyone will see it. And anyway, people already look at each other like that when meeting, glancing around as if their only concern was him. "Why would it be so, or maybe I'm just imagining things," he thought. There are fewer people there, and it's less noticeable, and in any case more convenient, and most importantly - further away from these places. And he was surprised suddenly: how did he spend whole half hours wandering in despair and anxiety, and in dangerous places, when this idea couldn't have occurred to him before! And that's why he only wasted whole half hours on a reckless business, which had already been decided upon in his sleep, in a delirium. He became extremely absent-minded and forgetful and knew it.
