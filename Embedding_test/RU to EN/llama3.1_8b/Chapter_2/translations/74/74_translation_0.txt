Then he tried to get up from the bed, wanted to run away, but someone always stopped him by force, and he again fell into powerlessness and unconsciousness. Finally, he completely regained his senses. This happened in the morning at ten o'clock. At this hour of the morning, on clear days, the sun always passed through a long strip across his right wall and lit up the corner near the door. By his bedside stood Nastasya and another man who was very curiously examining him and completely unknown to him. This was a young fellow in a caftan with a beard, and he looked like an artisan from afar. The landlady peered out of the half-opened door. Raskolnikov sat up. — Who is this, Nastasya? he asked, pointing at the young man.
