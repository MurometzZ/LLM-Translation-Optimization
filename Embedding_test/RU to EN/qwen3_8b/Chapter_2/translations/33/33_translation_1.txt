There they are, the writers!—and he cast a contemptuous glance at Raskolnikov.—Three days ago, there was an incident in a tavern: he had lunch, but refused to pay; "I'll describe you in satire for that," he said. On a ship, too, another incident occurred last week: a respected family of a state councilor, his wife and daughter, were insulted with the most offensive words. From the confectionery, they threw out one of us yesterday. That's what they are, writers, literati, students, heralds… damn it! Go away! I'll come myself to you… then be careful! Did you hear? Louise Ivanovna, with an insincerely polite manner, began to sit down everywhere, and while sitting down, she managed to reach the doors; but she bumped into a prominent officer with an open, fresh face and splendid, thick, white-blonde mustaches.
