"Oh, what the devil is all this! — he thought suddenly in a fit of boundless spite. — Well, it's started, so let it start, to hell with her and her new life! How stupid it is, Lord... And how much I've been babbling and flattering today! How disgusting I was and flirting with that scoundrel Ilya Petrovich yesterday! But what's the point of all this? I don't care about them or what I did. Not at all! Not at all!..."

Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question suddenly threw him off track and bitterly amazed him:

"If indeed everything was done consciously, not stupidly, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how on earth have you not even glanced into your wallet and don't know what you got, because of which you've been suffering all this time and deliberately went to such a vile, disgusting, low deed?"
