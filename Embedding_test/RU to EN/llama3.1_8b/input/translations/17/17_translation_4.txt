"If they ask me, I might say something," he thought, approaching the office. The office was a quarter of a verst away from him. It had just moved to a new apartment, in a new house, on the fourth floor. He had been there briefly once before, but very long ago. Entering through the gates, he saw a staircase to his right, down which a man with a book in his hands was walking: "a janitor, so it's here that the office is", and he began to climb up randomly. He didn't want to ask anyone anything about anything. "I'll go in, get on my knees and tell everything..." - he thought, entering the fourth floor. The staircase was narrow, steep, and full of filth. All kitchens from all apartments on all four floors opened onto this staircase and stood like that almost all day. That's why there was a terrible stench.
