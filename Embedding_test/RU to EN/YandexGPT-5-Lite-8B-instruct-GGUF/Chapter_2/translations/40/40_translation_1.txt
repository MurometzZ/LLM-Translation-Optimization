 The urge was so strong that he had already gotten up to act on it. “Shouldn’t I at least think about it for a minute?” ran through his head. “No, better not thinking about it and get it over with!” But suddenly he stopped as if struck dumb: Nikodim Fomich was talking heatedly to Ilya Petrovich, and words reached him:

— It can't be, both will be released! Firstly, everything contradicts; judge for yourself: why would they call a дворник if it were their business? To inform on themselves, perhaps? Or is it cunning? No, that would be too clever! And finally, student Pestyakov was seen near the gate by both the janitors and the commoner at the very moment he entered: he was walking with three friends and parted with them at the very gate and asked about living quarters from the janitors, even in front of his friends. Well, would someone like that ask about living quarters if they were going there with such an intention?
