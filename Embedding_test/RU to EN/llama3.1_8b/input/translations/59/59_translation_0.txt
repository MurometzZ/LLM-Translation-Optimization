Leave me alone! — Wait a minute, pipe-smoker! You're completely mad! As far as I'm concerned, you can do what you like. Look: I don't have any lessons, and I don't care anyway; but there's this bookseller Hervimov on Tolkuchy Street – that's a lesson in itself. I wouldn't exchange him for five merchant's lessons now. He publishes these little publications and natural science booklets – and how they sell! Some of the titles are just priceless! You've always said I'm stupid; by God, brother, there are people stupider than me! And he's useful too; he doesn't know what he's doing, but I encourage him naturally.
