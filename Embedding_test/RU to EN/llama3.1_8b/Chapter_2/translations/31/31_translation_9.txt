And how can you stick your head out of the window on the street like a little pig squealing; and that's shameful. Fie upon it, fie upon it, fie! And Karl behind him was pulling him by the tailcoat from the window, and then, this is true, sir captain, he tore his jacket. And then he shouted that he had to pay fifteen whole rubles as a fine, and I myself, sir captain, paid five whole rubles for him.
