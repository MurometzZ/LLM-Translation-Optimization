Though he could hardly think clearly at that moment, he felt that people would not treat him the same way when they came to take him. "But… the police?" — Would you like some tea? Anything you like? I'll bring it; there's still… — No… I'll go: I'll go right now," he muttered, standing up. "Go, and you won't even descend the stairs?" — I'll go… — As you like. She went after the janitor. Immediately he ran to the light to examine the sock and tassel: "There are stains, but not very noticeable; everything has gotten dirty, worn out, and already faded. Whoever doesn't know beforehand won't notice anything. Nastasya, therefore, couldn't have noticed anything from a distance, thank God!" Then, trembling, he opened the summons and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood.
