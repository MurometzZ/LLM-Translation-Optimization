Meanwhile, Razumikhin sat down on the couch next to him awkwardly, like a bear, wrapped his left hand around his head, despite the fact that he could have raised himself, and with his right hand brought a spoon of soup to his mouth, blowing on it several times beforehand to prevent him from burning. But the soup was only just warm. Raskolnikov greedily swallowed one spoonful, then another, the third. But after bringing several spoons, Razumikhin suddenly paused and announced that regarding further steps, they needed to consult with Zosimov. Nastasya entered, carrying two bottles of beer. — Do you want some tea? — I want. — Hurry up and make some tea, Nastasya, because, I think, regarding tea, you can do it without a faculty.
