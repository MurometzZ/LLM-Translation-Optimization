Then he would try to get up, wanting to run away, but someone always stopped him by force, and he would again fall into helplessness and confusion. Finally, he completely regained consciousness. This happened in the morning at ten o'clock. At this hour in clear days, the sun always cast a long stripe across his right wall, illuminating the corner by the door. Standing by his bed was Nastasia, and another person, who was curiously observing him and completely unfamiliar to him. He was a young man in a kaftan with a beard, and from his appearance, he looked like an artelshchik. The landlady peeked through the half-open door. Raskolnikov raised himself. — Who is this, Nastasia? — he asked, pointing at the young man.
