"By God, that's it! — he suddenly thought, seized by unrelenting wrath. — Well, here it begins, just as it should, let the devil take the new life! How foolish, Lord, how foolish!... How many lies I told and disgraced today! How vilely I flattered and played with the worst of them, Ilyá Petrovich! But in any case, that's nonsense too! I don't care about them all, nor about the fact that I flattered and played with them! It's completely different! It's completely different!..."
Suddenly he stopped; a new, completely unexpected and extremely simple question at once confused him and bitterly surprised him:
"If this whole matter was indeed done deliberately, not by accident, if you really had a definite and firm goal, then how come you haven't even looked into your purse so far and don't know what you've received, from which all these torments came, and why you deliberately went along with such a base, nasty, low act?"
