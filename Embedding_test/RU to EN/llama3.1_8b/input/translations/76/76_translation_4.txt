But the door swung open again at this moment, and Razzhivin, slightly bending because he was tall, entered. "What a sea cabin!" he shouted as he came in. "I always bump my forehead; it's called an apartment too! And you, brother, have you come to?" Nastasya replied, "Just now I've woken up." "We've just woken up," the arrester chimed in again with a smile. "And who are you yourself, then?" Razzhivin suddenly turned to him and asked.
