What have you got there, a book, is it? — A book, you see, here it is. — Give it here. Well, Rodja, get up. I'll hold you; give it to Raskolnikov, take the pen, brother, money is now sweeter than syrup. — No need, — said Raskolnikov, pushing the pen away. — Why not? — I won't sign. — Blazes, how can we do without a signature? — No need… money… — We don't need money at all!
