His entire room was of such a size that one could take off a hook without getting out of bed. So it is: the janitor and Nastasya are standing. Nastasya looked at him in some strange way. He looked at the janitor with a defiant and desperate expression. The janitor silently handed him a gray, folded paper sealed with a bottle-seal.
