Then he tried to get up, wanted to run away, but someone always stopped him by force, and he would again fall into helplessness and confusion. Finally, he completely regained consciousness. This happened in the morning, at ten o'clock. At this hour in clear days, the sun always passed along his right wall as a long strip, illuminating the corner by the door. Standing by his bed was Nastasya, and another person, who was very curiously observing him and completely unknown to him. He was a young man in a kaftan with a beard, and from the appearance, he looked like an artelshchik. The landlady peered in from the half-open door. Raskolnikov raised himself slightly. "Who is this, Nastasya?" he asked, pointing at the young man.
