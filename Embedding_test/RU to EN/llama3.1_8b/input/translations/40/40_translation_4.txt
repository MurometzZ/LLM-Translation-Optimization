The call was so strong that he had already risen from his seat to carry it out. "Shouldn't I think for a minute?" ran through his head. "No, better not think and be done with it!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking warmly to Il'ya Petrovich, and his words reached him:

— It cannot be that both will be released! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourselves: why would they call a janitor if it were their business? To inform on themselves, perhaps? Or out of cunning? No, it would be too cunning even! And finally, student Pestryakov was seen by both janitors and the woman at the very gates at the exact moment when he entered: he walked with three friends and parted from them right at the gates and asked about living arrangements from the janitors while his friends were still there. So, would he ask about living arrangements if he had such an intention?
