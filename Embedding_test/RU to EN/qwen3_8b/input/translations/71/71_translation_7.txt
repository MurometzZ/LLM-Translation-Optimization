He found it very unpleasant, even frightening. "Nastasya, why are you silent?" he finally whispered timidly. "This is blood," she finally answered, quietly, as if speaking to herself. "Blood!... Which blood?..." he muttered, pale and stepping back toward the wall. Nastasya continued to look at him silently. "No one beat the mistress," she said again in a strict and resolute voice. He looked at her, barely breathing.
