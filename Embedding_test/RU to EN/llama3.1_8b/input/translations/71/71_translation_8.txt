It became very unpleasant for him from this consideration, even frightening. — Nastasya, why are you silent? — he finally whispered weakly. — It's blood, — she replied quietly, as if to herself. — Blood! What blood?... — he muttered, paling and moving away from the wall. Nastasya continued to silently look at him. — No one beat the mistress, — she said again in a strict and resolute voice. He looked at her, barely breathing.
