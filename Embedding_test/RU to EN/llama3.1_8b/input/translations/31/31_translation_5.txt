And he was opening the window onto the canal and peering out of it like a little pig squealing; and this is shameful. And how can one peer out of the window onto the street like a little pig squealing; and this is shameful. Pshaw-pshaw-pshaw! And Karl behind him pulled his frock from the window, and then, I swear to you, captain, he tore off his jacket. And then he shouted that he had to pay fifteen whole marks in fines. And I myself, captain, paid him five whole marks for a torn jacket. And this is an impolite guest, captain, and he's causing all sorts of trouble! I'm saying, captain, you'll have big satire on me because I can write about you in all the newspapers. — From writers, then? — Yes, captain, and what kind of impolite guest are you, captain, when you're a guest in a noble house… — Ah-ah-ah!
