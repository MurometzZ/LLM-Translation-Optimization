The hot lieutenant was visibly perplexed. — This is none of your business! — he finally shouted somehow unnaturally loudly, — but do show him, Alexander Grigorievich, the complaints that are being made against you! Don't pay any money! Look how well you've flown the coop! But Raskolnikov was no longer listening and greedily grabbed for the paper, trying to quickly figure out the riddle.
