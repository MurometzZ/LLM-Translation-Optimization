— Yes, Mr. Captain, and what an unworthy guest, Mr. Captain, when in a noble home…
— Well, well, well! Enough! I've already told you, told you, I've told you…
— Colonel Ilyich! — the clerk spoke again significantly. The lieutenant quickly glanced at him; the clerk slightly nodded his head. — …Here you go, esteemed Lady Laviza Ivanovna, my final word, and this is for the last time, — the lieutenant continued. — If there is another scandal in your noble home, even just once more, I'll take you to the very essence of a cugunder, as it's said in high style. Did you hear? So a writer, a storyteller, took five groschen in "The Noble House" for a cloak? There they are, the writers!
