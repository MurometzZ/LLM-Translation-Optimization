It seemed to him that he had cut himself off from everyone and everything at that moment. He came to himself by evening, which meant he had walked for only six hours. He didn't remember where or how he had walked back. Undressing and trembling all over, like a driven horse, he lay on the sofa, put on his overcoat, and fell asleep at once... 

He awoke in the depths of dusk from a terrible scream. Goodness, what a scream! Such unnatural sounds, such howling, screaming, screeching, tears, brawling, and curses he had never heard or seen before. He could not imagine such cruelty, such frenzy. In fear, he raised himself and sat on his bed, trembling and suffering every moment. But the fights, screams, and curses became stronger and stronger. And here, to his greatest amazement, he suddenly heard his mistress's voice.
