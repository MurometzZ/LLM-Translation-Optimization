— Do not shout! — I am not shouting, but speaking very calmly, and it is you who are shouting at me; I am a student, and I will not allow myself to be shouted at. The assistant had become so enraged that for the first moment he could not utter a word, and only some sprays were coming out of his mouth. He jumped up from his seat. — Do you dare be quiet! You are in the presence. Do not behave like a rude person, sir! — Yes, and you are in the presence, — cried Raskolnikov, — and besides, you are shouting and smoking a cigarette, so you are showing disrespect to all of us. — Having said this, Raskolnikov felt an indescribable pleasure. The clerk looked at them with a smile.
