The call was so strong that he had already risen from his seat to act on it. "Shouldn't I think for a minute?" flashed through his head. "No, better not think and off with it!" But suddenly he stopped as if rooted to the spot: Nikodim Fomich was speaking warmly to Il'ya Petrovich, and his words reached him:

— It's impossible that both will be released! First of all, everything contradicts; judge for yourselves: why would they summon a janitor if it were their business? To inform on themselves, perhaps? Or for cunning? No, it would be too cunning! And finally, the student Pestryakov was seen by both janitors and the housewife at the very gates at that exact moment when he entered: he walked with three friends and parted from them right at the gates and asked about living arrangements from the janitors while his friends were still there. So would he ask about living arrangements if he had such an intention?
