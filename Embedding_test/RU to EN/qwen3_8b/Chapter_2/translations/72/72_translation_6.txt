He stared at her, scarcely breathing. "I heard it myself... I didn't sleep... I was sitting," he said even more timidly. "I listened for a long time... The assistant overseer came... Everyone ran down the stairs, from all the apartments..." "No one came. But your blood is crying out. That's when she has no way out and her liver begins to burn—then hallucinations start... Are you going to eat at all?" He didn't answer. Nastasya stood over him, intently gazing at him and didn't leave. "Give me something to drink... Nastasyushka." She went downstairs and returned two minutes later with water in a white clay cup; but he no longer remembered what came next. He remembered only how he took a sip of cold water and spilled it from the cup onto his chest.
