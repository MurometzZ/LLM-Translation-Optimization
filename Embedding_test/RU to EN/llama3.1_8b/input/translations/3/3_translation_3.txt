What's this? He immediately rushed to take them out and throw them onto the table. Having taken everything, even rummaging through pockets to make sure nothing was left behind, he carried all of it over to the corner. There, in the very corner, at the bottom, in one place, the torn-off wallpaper from the wall had been ripped: immediately he began stuffing everything into this hole under a sheet of paper: "it's gone! Everything is out of sight and even the wallet too!" - he thought joyfully, standing up and stupidly staring into the corner, at the further enlarged hole. Suddenly he shuddered with horror: "God have mercy," he whispered in despair, "what's happening to me? Is it really hidden? Do they hide things like this?"
