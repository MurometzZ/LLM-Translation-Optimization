Nastasya stood over him, staring at him intently and not leaving. — Give me a drink... Nastasyushka. She went down and came back up two minutes later with water in a white clay cup; but he already didn't remember what happened next. He only remembered taking one sip of cold water and spilling it from the cup onto his chest.
