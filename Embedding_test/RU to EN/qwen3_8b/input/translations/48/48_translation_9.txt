What if the cases don't sink but float? Yes, of course. Everyone will see. And already, they all look at them, meeting, glancing, as if it's all about them. "Why not, or perhaps it's just me," he thought. Finally, it occurred to him that perhaps it would be better to go somewhere on the Neva. There are fewer people, it's less noticeable, and in any case more convenient, and mainly farther from these places. And he suddenly realized: how could he have walked for half an hour in melancholy and anxiety, and in dangerous places, and couldn't think of this before! And that's why he spent half an hour on a reckless thing, because it was already decided once in a dream, in a delirium! He became extremely distracted and forgetful and knew it.
