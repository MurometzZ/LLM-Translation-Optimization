Nastasya, thank God, could not notice anything from afar! He carefully unsealed the notice and began to read; he read for a long time and finally understood. It was a regular notice from the quarter to appear today at half past ten in the office of the quarter's supervisor. "When did this ever happen? I have no dealings with the police on my own! And why exactly today?" he thought in agonizing confusion. "Lord, hurry up!" He was about to kneel down to pray, but even he laughed at himself—not at the prayer, but at himself. He hurried to get dressed. "I'll be gone one way or another! Put on a sock!"
